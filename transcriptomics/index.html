BLOCK 2 · 轉錄體學

轉錄體學總覽

RNA 是「基因被打開後實際生產的東西」——從組織平均、到單細胞、到保留空間結構，三個解析度層次。

RNA is what cells actually produce after genes turn on — across three resolutions: tissue average, single-cell, and space-preserving.

WHY

① 為什麼轉錄體學？

DNA 是「劇本」，RNA 是「正在演出的台詞」。轉錄體告訴你細胞當下「實際在做什麼」、哪些基因被打開、哪些被關閉——這是體學分析最常見的入口。

DNA is the script; RNA is the lines currently being spoken. Transcriptomics tells you what cells are doing right now — which genes are on, which are off. It's the most common entry point to omics.
