Bulk RNA-seq · STAR / HISAT2 記憶體

教學中標示 STAR 約需 30 GB、HISAT2 約 8 GB 記憶體。這是人類 GRCh38 + GENCODE 註釋下的典型值；對較小基因組（如酵母、果蠅）兩者記憶體都顯著降低。HISAT2 自 2.2 版起的 graph genome 模式可能更耗記憶體，與標準 reference 模式不同。

The tutorial cites about 30 GB for STAR and about 8 GB for HISAT2. These are typical numbers for human GRCh38 with GENCODE annotation; for smaller genomes (yeast, fly) both fall substantially. From version 2.2 onward HISAT2's graph genome mode can use more memory than the standard reference mode.

來源：STAR manual；HISAT2 manual。

Sources: STAR manual; HISAT2 manual.

Bulk RNA-seq · MACS2 vs MACS3

教學在 ATAC peak calling 章節並列了 MACS2/3。實務上自 2022 年起 MACS3 已為官方主線（pip install macs3），MACS2 進入維護模式但仍廣泛使用。新分析建議直接用 MACS3，且須注意 --shift / --extsize 與舊版預設行為的細微差異。

The ATAC peak-calling section lists MACS2 and MACS3 side by side. In practice MACS3 has been the official mainline since 2022 (pip install macs3), while MACS2 is in maintenance mode but still widely used. New analyses should default to MACS3, taking care that --shift / --extsize defaults differ subtly from the older version.

來源：macs3-project/MACS GitHub release notes。

Source: macs3-project/MACS GitHub release notes.

補充說明 · Notes

Bulk RNA-seq · 「DEG 定義 padj < 0.05 且 |LFC| > 1」

教學提到「業界慣例」DEG = padj < 0.05 且 |LFC| > 1。這是常見啟發式門檻，但並非統計上嚴格的「DE」定義——DESeq2 / edgeR 的顯著性是基於是否拒絕「LFC = 0」假設，與 |LFC| > 1 是兩個獨立的條件。Love 等人建議若想以 LFC 大小為門檻，應在統計檢定時加入 lfcThreshold 參數，而非後選 (post-hoc filtering)。

The tutorial calls DEG = padj < 0.05 and |LFC| > 1 an "industry convention." This is a common heuristic, but it is not a statistically rigorous definition of DE — DESeq2 / edgeR significance is based on rejecting the null of LFC = 0, which is an independent condition from |LFC| > 1. Love et al. recommend that if you want an LFC magnitude threshold, supply it via the lfcThreshold argument during testing rather than as post-hoc filtering.

來源：Love et al. 2014；DESeq2 vignette「Tests of log2 fold change above or below a threshold」一節。

Sources: Love et al. 2014; DESeq2 vignette section "Tests of log2 fold change above or below a threshold".

Spatial · Visium HD 解析度

教學標示 Visium HD 解析度為 2 μm。技術上 Visium HD 的 capture area 由 2 × 2 μm 的 squares 鋪滿（即每 bin 2 μm），但實際單細胞識別仍受核大小限制（典型核 5-10 μm），且 spot signal 可能涵蓋多個 bin。稱「2 μm 解析度」是 nominal 規格，達到單細胞層級需配合分割演算法。

The tutorial lists Visium HD resolution as 2 μm. Technically the Visium HD capture area is tiled with 2 × 2 μm squares (so each bin is 2 μm), but single-cell identification is still limited by nuclear size (typically 5–10 μm), and a spot's signal can span multiple bins. The "2 μm resolution" figure is a nominal specification; reaching the single-cell level requires segmentation algorithms.

Bulk RNA-seq · Salmon vs kallisto 偏差校正

教學常將 Salmon 與 kallisto 視為「等價的 pseudo-alignment 工具」。實務上 Salmon 預設啟用 GC bias（--gcBias）、sequence bias（--seqBias）與 positional bias（--posBias）等偏差校正模型，而 kallisto 並無對應選項。對於 polyA-selected 樣品差異不大，但對 FFPE 或退化 RNA 樣品，Salmon 的偏差校正可顯著改變 transcript-level 估計值，這在低品質樣品差異表現分析中尤其重要 (Patro 2017)。

Tutorials often treat Salmon and kallisto as "equivalent pseudo-alignment tools." In practice Salmon's defaults enable GC bias (--gcBias), sequence bias (--seqBias) and positional bias (--posBias) correction models, whereas kallisto has no equivalent flags. The gap is small for polyA-selected libraries, but for FFPE or degraded-RNA samples Salmon's bias correction can shift transcript-level estimates substantially, which matters most for DE analysis on low-quality material (Patro 2017).

來源：Patro et al. 2017, Nat Methods。

Source: Patro et al. 2017, Nat Methods.

補充說明 · Notes

ATAC-seq · Tn5 9-bp 偏差與 TOBIAS footprinting

教學雖提及 ATAC-seq 的 --shift -75 --extsize 150，但較少強調 Tn5 轉位酶具有內建的 9-bp 序列偏好性（兩個 monomer 間隔 9 bp 切斷 DNA）。這在 footprinting 分析中會造成假陽性 TF 結合訊號。TOBIAS (Bentsen 2020) 採用 ATACorrect 模組對 Tn5 9-bp 偏差建模並校正，是當前 differential footprinting 的推薦做法；HINT-ATAC 與 RGT 亦有類似校正。原始未校正的 footprint score 在 motif 富集分析中可能誤導下游解釋。

The tutorial mentions ATAC-seq's --shift -75 --extsize 150 but rarely highlights that the Tn5 transposase has a built-in 9-bp sequence preference (two monomers cut DNA 9 bp apart). In footprinting analyses this produces false-positive TF binding signals. TOBIAS (Bentsen 2020) models and corrects the Tn5 9-bp bias via its ATACorrect module and is the current recommendation for differential footprinting; HINT-ATAC and RGT use similar corrections. Uncorrected raw footprint scores can mislead downstream motif-enrichment interpretation.

來源：Bentsen et al. 2020, Nat Commun。

Source: Bentsen et al. 2020, Nat Commun.

補充說明 · Notes

Variant calling · GATK Best Practices 版本漂移與 DeepVariant

教學引用 GATK Best Practices 時常忽略 「Best Practices」是 Broad 持續更新的活文件，而非凍結版本。例如 HaplotypeCaller 在 GATK4 後 default 行為與 GATK3 已不同（PCR-free 預設、AS-annotation、CNN filtering 取代 VQSR for small cohorts）；許多舊論文/教學沿用的 VariantFiltration 硬閾值在新版可能不再為官方推薦。替代方案 DeepVariant（Poplin 2018, Nat Biotechnol）採用 CNN 直接從 pileup image 預測 genotype，在 Illumina 與 PacBio HiFi 上常優於 GATK，且不需 BQSR / VQSR 步驟，是 2020 年後的競爭主流。

Citations to GATK Best Practices often overlook that "Best Practices" is a living document maintained by Broad, not a frozen specification. HaplotypeCaller's defaults shifted between GATK3 and GATK4 (PCR-free defaults, AS-annotations, CNN filtering replacing VQSR for small cohorts), and many hard VariantFiltration thresholds copied from older tutorials are no longer the official recommendation. DeepVariant (Poplin 2018, Nat Biotechnol) replaces this stack with a CNN that predicts genotypes directly from pileup images, often outperforming GATK on Illumina and PacBio HiFi, and skips BQSR / VQSR — making it a mainstream alternative since 2020.

來源：Poplin et al. 2018, Nat Biotechnol；GATK Best Practices Workflows。

Sources: Poplin et al. 2018, Nat Biotechnol; GATK Best Practices Workflows.

補充說明 · Notes

Alignment · BWA-MEM2 vs BWA-MEM 記憶體成本

教學常以「BWA-MEM2 比 BWA-MEM 快 ~2×」一語帶過。速度提升的代價是記憶體使用顯著上升：BWA-MEM 對人類基因組約需 ~5 GB index，而 BWA-MEM2 的 BWT 加速索引（.bwt.2bit.64）需 ~38 GB（v2.0）或 ~28 GB（v2.2 後使用更緊湊的 index）。在 HPC 節點 RAM 充裕時無問題，但 32 GB 筆電/工作站可能直接無法載入索引。對 capacity-constrained 環境應評估是否值得換取速度 (Vasimuddin 2019)。

Tutorials often drop in "BWA-MEM2 is ~2× faster than BWA-MEM" without context. The speed-up comes with a substantially larger memory footprint: BWA-MEM needs about 5 GB of index for the human genome, while BWA-MEM2's BWT-accelerated index (.bwt.2bit.64) needs about 38 GB (v2.0) or roughly 28 GB (v2.2+ with the more compact index). That is fine on RAM-rich HPC nodes, but a 32 GB laptop or workstation may simply fail to load the index. Capacity-constrained setups should weigh the trade-off before switching (Vasimuddin 2019).

來源：Vasimuddin et al. 2019, IPDPS；bwa-mem2 GitHub release notes。

Sources: Vasimuddin et al. 2019, IPDPS; bwa-mem2 GitHub release notes.

補充說明 · Notes

Microbiome · 16S V3-V4 屬層級 vs WGS 株層級

教學提到 16S rRNA 定序可「鑑定到種」。實際解析度大幅受限於擴增區段：V3-V4（最常見的 ~460 bp 區段）通常僅能可靠到屬（genus）層級，種（species）僅在部分 well-characterized clade 可分（Johnson 2019）。全長 16S（V1-V9）配合 PacBio HiFi 可達種，但仍難分株（strain）。要在株層級解析功能或抗藥性基因，需做 shotgun metagenomics（WGS）並以 MetaPhlAn4 / StrainPhlAn / inStrain 等工具分析。把 16S 結果在 species 層級當作 ground truth 是常見誤用。

Tutorials often state that 16S rRNA sequencing can "identify to species." The realistic resolution is heavily limited by the amplified region: V3-V4 (the most common ~460 bp window) is generally reliable only to genus, with species-level calls feasible only for a few well-characterized clades (Johnson 2019). Full-length 16S (V1-V9) on PacBio HiFi reaches species but still cannot resolve strains. For strain-level function or antimicrobial-resistance analysis, shotgun metagenomics (WGS) with MetaPhlAn4 / StrainPhlAn / inStrain is required. Treating 16S species-level calls as ground truth is a common misuse.

來源：Johnson et al. 2019, Nat Commun。

Source: Johnson et al. 2019, Nat Commun.

補充說明 · Notes

Spatial · 細胞分割的注意事項（Baysor）

教學常將「分割（segmentation）」視為單純的 DAPI / membrane 影像處理問題。但 MERFISH / Xenium 等 imaging-based spatial 技術的細胞邊界並非由影像直接決定——transcript 可能落在細胞外（diffusion、tissue tear），單純依 nuclei 擴張會誤分。Baysor (Petukhov 2022, Nat Biotechnol) 提出以 transcript 空間密度為主、影像為先驗 (prior) 的聯合分割，較傳統 Watershed / Cellpose-on-DAPI 更能處理 cytoplasmic transcript 與 dense neuropil。下游分析應檢視 fraction of assigned vs unassigned transcripts、以及 segmentation method 對細胞型別比例的影響。

Tutorials often treat "segmentation" as a pure DAPI / membrane image-processing problem. But cell boundaries in imaging-based spatial assays (MERFISH, Xenium) are not determined by the image alone — transcripts can fall outside cells (diffusion, tissue tears), so naive nuclear-expansion masks misassign them. Baysor (Petukhov 2022, Nat Biotechnol) proposes a joint segmentation that uses transcript spatial density as the primary signal and the image as a prior, handling cytoplasmic transcripts and dense neuropil better than Watershed or Cellpose-on-DAPI. Downstream analyses should report the fraction of assigned vs unassigned transcripts and check how the segmentation method shifts cell-type proportions.

來源：Petukhov et al. 2022, Nat Biotechnol。

Source: Petukhov et al. 2022, Nat Biotechnol.
