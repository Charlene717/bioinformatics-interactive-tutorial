總覽

兩大路線

🔬

16S / ITS Amplicon

PCR 擴增 16S rRNA (細菌) 或 ITS (真菌) 的可變區，定序後分類。優：便宜、樣本量大。限：只到 genus / species 級、無功能資訊。

PCR-amplify 16S rRNA (bacteria) or ITS (fungi) variable regions, sequence, classify. Pro: cheap, scalable. Con: genus/species resolution at best; no function info.

🧬

Shotgun Metagenomics

隨機定序所有 DNA，含全基因組覆蓋。優：strain-level、含功能基因、可組裝 MAG。限：每樣本成本高、計算量大。

Random sequencing of all DNA. Pro: strain-level, functional genes, MAG assembly. Con: high per-sample cost, compute-heavy.

關鍵概念

必懂三個概念

Alpha diversity

單一樣本內物種豐富度與均勻度。常用：Shannon、Simpson、Faith's PD。

Within-sample richness + evenness. Common: Shannon, Simpson, Faith's PD.

Beta diversity

樣本間群落結構差異。常用：Bray-Curtis、UniFrac (weighted/unweighted)。

Between-sample compositional differences. Common: Bray-Curtis, UniFrac (weighted/unweighted).

Compositionality

微生物相對豐度為「比例資料」，總和為 1。傳統統計（t-test、Pearson r）會誤導；應用 CLR 轉換 + ALDEx2 / ANCOM。

Microbial relative abundances are compositional (sum to 1). Traditional stats (t-test, Pearson) mislead; use CLR + ALDEx2 / ANCOM.
