微生物群體學

從 16S 條碼到 shotgun 全基因組組裝、從「誰在裡面」到「他們在做什麼」——揭開人體與環境微生物群落的組成、功能與生態。

From 16S barcoding to shotgun whole-genome assembly, from "who is there" to "what they do" — uncovering composition, function, and ecology of microbial communities in human and environmental contexts.

為什麼重要

為什麼微生物群體學正在改寫醫學？

你身上的細胞中，只有約一半是「你」的人類細胞。另一半屬於 38 兆個細菌、古菌、真菌與病毒——它們的基因總和 (metagenome「meta-」(超越) + genome：一個樣本中所有微生物 DNA 的總和，可以幾百萬個基因，遠超人類基因組 2 萬個。) 比人類基因組多 100 倍以上。這些微生物製造神經傳導物質、分解纖維、訓練免疫系統、甚至影響你對藥物的反應。

近十年的關鍵發現包括：

  • 糞菌移植 (FMT) 治療 Clostridioides difficile 復發性感染達 90% 治癒率，2022 年首個 FMT 製劑 (Rebyota) 獲 FDA 核准。
  • 癌症免疫治療反應：黑色素瘤接受 anti-PD1 治療的患者中，腸道有 Akkermansia muciniphila、Faecalibacterium 者反應率高 3 倍 (Routy 2018, Gopalakrishnan 2018)。
  • 發炎性腸道疾病 (IBD)：HMP2 (Lloyd-Price 2019, Nature) 顯示克隆氏症患者腸道 Faecalibacterium prausnitzii 銳減、產生丁酸的細菌減少、Proteobacteria 過度增生。
  • 腸—腦軸：迷走神經 + 微生物代謝物 (SCFA、色胺酸代謝物、GABA、5-HT) 影響憂鬱、自閉症、帕金森氏症。
  • 抗生素抗藥性 (AMR)：腸道是水平基因轉移的「軍火庫」，shotgun 可即時偵測 ESBL、CRE、CARD 資料庫中的抗性基因。

About half of the cells in your body are not human. The other half are 38 trillion bacteria, archaea, fungi, and viruses — whose collective genes (the metagenome"meta-" (beyond) + genome: the total DNA of all microbes in a sample — millions of genes, 100× more than the human genome (~20,000).) outnumber human genes 100-fold. These microbes synthesize neurotransmitters, ferment fiber, train the immune system, and even shape drug response.

Landmark findings of the last decade:

  • Fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT) cures ~90% of recurrent Clostridioides difficile infections; in 2022 the first FMT product (Rebyota) was FDA-approved.
  • Cancer immunotherapy response: in melanoma patients on anti-PD1, those with gut Akkermansia muciniphila or Faecalibacterium respond 3× more often (Routy 2018, Gopalakrishnan 2018).
  • Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD): HMP2 (Lloyd-Price 2019, Nature) showed Crohn's patients lose Faecalibacterium prausnitzii, lose butyrate producers, and gain Proteobacteria blooms.
  • Gut–brain axis: vagal nerve + microbial metabolites (SCFAs, tryptophan derivatives, GABA, 5-HT) influence depression, autism spectrum, Parkinson's.
  • Antimicrobial resistance (AMR): the gut is a horizontal-gene-transfer arsenal; shotgun sequencing can flag ESBL, CRE, and CARD-listed resistance genes in real time.
比喻： 如果把人體想成一座城市，每個器官就是一個社區。腸道是「人口最多的市中心」(10^11 cells/g)，皮膚是「沿海港口」(乾、油、濕三種氣候帶)，口腔是「24 小時夜市」(快速代謝、菌斑生態)。微生物群體學就是這座城市的「人口普查 + 經濟產業調查」。 Picture the body as a city; each organ is a district. The gut is "downtown" (10^11 cells/g), skin is "the harbor" (dry, oily, moist zones), the mouth is "the 24-hour night market" (fast metabolism, plaque ecology). Microbiome science is the census and the industrial survey of that city.
兩大策略

兩大實驗策略

🔬

16S / ITS Amplicon

用一段「保守—可變」交替的 ribosomal RNA核醣體 RNA 基因。16S (細菌與古菌)、18S (真核)、ITS (Internal Transcribed Spacer，真菌常用)。 基因當條碼。PCR 擴增 → 定序 → 分類。

優：$15–40/樣本，可平行做幾百個樣本；對低生物量樣本 (皮膚、腦組織) 仍可分析。

限：多到 genus 解析；無法看功能；PCR 偏差影響定量；不能偵測病毒、質體、噬菌體。

Use a "conserved–variable" interleaved ribosomal RNARibosomal RNA gene: 16S (bacteria/archaea), 18S (eukaryotes), ITS (Internal Transcribed Spacer, common for fungi). gene as a barcode. PCR-amplify → sequence → classify.

Pro: $15–40/sample, hundreds of samples in parallel; works on low-biomass samples (skin, brain).

Con: genus-level resolution at best; no function; PCR bias affects quantification; cannot detect viruses, plasmids, phages.

🧬

Shotgun Metagenomics

無偏隨機定序所有 DNA：細菌、古菌、真菌、病毒、質體、宿主皆混在一起。

優：strain 級解析；功能基因 (KEGG、CAZymes、AMR)；可組裝 MAGMetagenome-Assembled Genome：從 metagenomic reads 組裝、再分箱 (binning) 拼回的「準完整基因組」，可達 90%+ 完整度。；偵測病毒體 (virome) 與抗藥基因。

限：$100–250/樣本；計算量 100–1000× 大；宿主 DNA 干擾 (糞便 ~5%、口腔 ~50%、組織 > 90%)。

Unbiased random sequencing of all DNA: bacteria, archaea, fungi, viruses, plasmids, host — all mixed.

Pro: strain-level resolution; functional genes (KEGG, CAZymes, AMR); can assemble MAGsMetagenome-Assembled Genomes: near-complete genomes reconstructed by assembling and binning metagenomic reads — 90%+ completeness possible.; detects viromes and resistomes.

Con: $100–250/sample; 100–1000× more compute; host DNA contamination (stool ~5%, oral ~50%, tissue >90%).

🔬 Pick 16S/ITS when…
  • 預算 < $50/樣本、樣本量大 (cohort > 200)
  • 只需要描述群落結構 (alpha/beta diversity)
  • 低生物量樣本 (皮膚、組織活檢、植物根際)
  • 需與既有 16S 公開資料比較
  • Budget < $50/sample, large cohort (>200)
  • Only describing community structure (alpha/beta)
  • Low-biomass samples (skin, biopsy, plant rhizosphere)
  • Comparing with existing 16S public data
VS
🧬 Pick Shotgun when…
  • 需要 species 或 strain 級分辨
  • 研究功能 (代謝、AMR、毒力因子)
  • 要重建 MAG、找新菌
  • 研究病毒體 (virome) 或真菌體 (mycobiome)
  • Need species / strain resolution
  • Studying function (metabolism, AMR, virulence)
  • Reconstructing MAGs, discovering novel taxa
  • Studying virome or mycobiome

核心觀念

五個核心概念

微生物群體分析的五支柱
① Alpha diversity

單一樣本內豐富度 (richness) + 均勻度 (evenness)。Shannon、Simpson、Chao1、Faith's PD。

Within-sample richness + evenness. Shannon, Simpson, Chao1, Faith's PD.

② Beta diversity

樣本間差異。Bray-Curtis、Jaccard、UniFrac (weighted/unweighted)。需配 PERMANOVA。

Between-sample dissimilarity. Bray-Curtis, Jaccard, UniFrac (weighted/unweighted). Pair with PERMANOVA.

③ Compositionality

資料是「比例」(總和=1)，傳統 t-test 失效。CLR + ALDEx2 / ANCOM-BC 才對。

Data are proportions (sum = 1). T-test misleads. Use CLR + ALDEx2 / ANCOM-BC.

④ Sparsity

90%+ 的 ASV/OTU 計數是 0。需要零值處理、過濾、或專用模型 (zero-inflated)。

90%+ of ASV/OTU counts are 0. Needs zero handling, filtering, or zero-inflated models.

⑤ Function ≠ Taxonomy

同物種不同 strain 帶不同基因；不同物種卻可能有同基因 (HGT)。功能必須直接量。

Same species, different strains carry different genes; unrelated species share genes (HGT). Function must be measured directly.

重大陷阱

開始前必知的 5 個陷阱

① Kitome： DNA 抽取套組、PCR 試劑本身就帶微量微生物 DNA。低生物量樣本 (皮膚、血液、胎盤) 可能 90% 訊號來自試劑。對策：永遠跑 negative controls (空 buffer)，用 decontam R 套件對照刪除。 DNA extraction kits and PCR reagents harbor trace microbial DNA. In low-biomass samples (skin, blood, placenta) 90% of signal can be reagent-derived. Always run negative controls (blank buffer) and remove with the decontam R package.
② Batch： 同一個樣本由不同操作員、不同抽取套組、不同定序 lane 跑，beta diversity 差異 > 真實生物差異。對策：在實驗設計就讓 case/control 隨機分散到每個 batch；分析時用 ConQuR、combat-seq。 Same sample processed by different operator / kit / lane can have larger beta-diversity than true biology. Block by randomizing case/control across batches in design; correct with ConQuR, combat-seq.
③ 統計失敗： 微生物資料是 compositional (總和為 1)：A 升高必然讓其他「看起來」下降。直接 t-test / wilcoxon 會產生假陽性。對策：CLR 變換 + ALDEx2 / ANCOM-BC。 Microbiome data are compositional (sum = 1): when A increases, others must "appear" to decrease. Naive t-test/Wilcoxon yields false positives. Use CLR transform + ALDEx2 / ANCOM-BC.
④ Primer 偏差： V1-V2 對皮膚 Cutibacterium 失明；V4 漏 Bifidobacterium；ITS1 vs ITS2 對真菌結果不同。對策：先看你目標 taxa 在所選 primer 下的 in-silico 覆蓋。 V1-V2 misses skin Cutibacterium; V4 misses Bifidobacterium; ITS1 vs ITS2 give different fungal results. Check in-silico coverage of your target taxa under your primer.
⑤ 因果陷阱： 「IBD 患者 Faecalibacterium 少」≠「Faecalibacterium 缺失導致 IBD」。可能是發炎環境抑制了它。對策：gnotobiotic mouse 移植驗證、Mendelian randomization、縱向 cohort、機制實驗。 "IBD patients have less Faecalibacterium" ≠ "loss of Faecalibacterium causes IBD". Inflammation may suppress it. Validate with gnotobiotic mice, Mendelian randomization, longitudinal cohorts, mechanistic experiments.
