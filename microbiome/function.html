為什麼重要

為什麼功能比物種重要？

2019 年 Nature 上的 HMP2 IBD 研究 (Lloyd-Price et al.) 提供了「物種 vs 功能」最戲劇性的對比。在 132 位 IBD 患者 vs 健康人縱向追蹤中：

  • 物種變化：alpha diversity 下降，Faecalibacterium prausnitzii、Roseburia 減少；Proteobacteria 增加。但個體之間差異很大，難以建構診斷模型。
  • 功能變化：丁酸合成路徑 (butyrate via butyryl-CoA acetate CoA transferase) 顯著下降；色胺酸代謝路徑 (kynurenine pathway 上升、indole 下降) 重組；硫化物代謝增強。功能訊號比物種訊號更穩定，可作為跨研究 IBD biomarker。

同樣的觀察在多個系統重複：

  • 癌症免疫治療：response 與否更與「多胺合成、inosine 產生、2'-FL 利用」這些功能關聯，而非單一菌種 (Sivan, Routy 2015–2023)。
  • 第二型糖尿病：菌種變異大，但短鏈脂肪酸 (SCFA) 合成下降、BCAA 合成上升幾乎一致 (Karlsson 2013, Pedersen 2016)。
  • 新生兒：剖腹產 vs 自然產的 strain 差異大，但「複合醣分解」與「免疫訓練」相關功能相似化只需幾個月。

核心訊息：功能比物種更接近表型 (phenotype)。同樣，物種比基因更穩定——你研究的是哪一層，決定了用哪個工具。

The 2019 Nature HMP2 IBD study (Lloyd-Price et al.) offers the cleanest "species vs function" contrast. In 132 IBD patients vs healthy with longitudinal sampling:

  • Species changes: alpha diversity drops, Faecalibacterium prausnitzii and Roseburia fall; Proteobacteria rise. But between-individual variance is huge — poor for diagnostic models.
  • Function changes: butyrate synthesis (via butyryl-CoA acetate CoA transferase) is significantly down; tryptophan metabolism reroutes (kynurenine up, indole down); sulfide metabolism up. Function signal is more stable than species and serves as cross-study IBD biomarkers.

The same pattern recurs across systems:

  • Cancer immunotherapy: response correlates with polyamine synthesis, inosine production, 2'-FL utilization — not a single species (Sivan, Routy 2015–2023).
  • Type 2 diabetes: species variable, but SCFA synthesis falls and BCAA synthesis rises consistently (Karlsson 2013, Pedersen 2016).
  • Neonates: huge strain differences between C-section and vaginal delivery, but functional convergence (carbohydrate degradation, immune training) emerges within months.

Core message: function is closer to phenotype than species. And species are more stable than genes — which layer you study dictates which tool you use.

比喻： 把腸道想成交響樂團。16S / shotgun 物種告訴你「樂團裡有幾把小提琴、幾把大提琴」(誰)；功能分析告訴你「他們今晚在演奏什麼樂章」(做什麼)。兩個樂團演奏同一首貝多芬，可能有不同的樂器組合——同樣的「健康功能」可以由不同菌組組合達成。這就是為什麼 IBD 病人「物種變了，但功能變得更類似」。 Think of the gut as a symphony orchestra. 16S / shotgun species tells you "how many violins, cellos, oboes" (who); function analysis tells you "what they are playing tonight" (what). Two orchestras can perform the same Beethoven with different instruments — likewise, the same "healthy function" can be realized by different species mixes. That's why IBD patients can have "different species but more similar dysfunction".
兩條路

兩條路

📚 Shotgun 直接量化

把 reads 比對到蛋白家族 (UniRef、KEGG Orthology、Pfam、TIGRFAM、eggNOG)。實際存在的基因——最準確。

優：真實量化、可達 strain、能找 AMR / 毒力。

限：需要 shotgun (貴) + 大型計算。

工具：HUMAnN3 (標準)、SqueezeMeta、metaG-Tk、eggNOG-mapper、KofamScan。

Align reads to protein families (UniRef, KEGG Orthology, Pfam, TIGRFAM, eggNOG). Actual genes — most accurate.

Pro: true quantification, can reach strain level, finds AMR / virulence.

Con: requires shotgun (expensive) + heavy compute.

Tools: HUMAnN3 (standard), SqueezeMeta, metaG-Tk, eggNOG-mapper, KofamScan.

VS
🔮 16S 推估

用已知物種的「基因組功能輪廓」推估 16S 樣本可能有什麼基因。是「猜」，依賴參考基因組覆蓋。

優：16S 預算就能跑、結果速度快、cohort 規模大也可。

限：失準於非培養菌；對 strain 級功能差異盲；不可作機制論述。

工具：PICRUSt2 (主流)、Tax4Fun2、Piphillin、PanFP。

Predict gene content of a 16S sample from known reference genomes. It's a guess, dependent on reference coverage.

Pro: runs on a 16S budget, fast, scales to huge cohorts.

Con: wrong for uncultured organisms; blind to strain-level function; should not drive mechanistic claims.

Tools: PICRUSt2 (main), Tax4Fun2, Piphillin, PanFP.

HUMAnN3 機制

HUMAnN3 三層架構

HUMAnN3 (HMP Unified Metabolic Analysis Network 3) 是 shotgun 功能分析的事實標準。三層 tiered search 策略：

  1. Tier 1 — MetaPhlAn4 物種快篩：先找出樣本中哪些物種存在 (clade-specific marker genes)。
  2. Tier 2 — Bowtie2 對 ChocoPhlAn pangenome：對已偵測物種，拉出它們的 pangenome；reads bowtie2 對齊。多數 reads (typically > 60%) 在此匹配，並能追蹤到物種。
  3. Tier 3 — translated DIAMOND 對 UniRef90：未匹配 reads 翻譯成蛋白後跑 DIAMOND (BLASTP 加速版)。能找到稀有物種或新基因。
  4. 輸出三層表：① genefamilies (UniRef90，可 stratified by species)、② pathabundance (MetaCyc pathway)、③ pathcoverage。

Stratification 是 killer feature：HUMAnN3 不只說「丁酸合成路徑下降」，還告訴你「下降來自 Roseburia 30% + F. prausnitzii 40% + unclassified 30%」。臨床機制研究關鍵。

HUMAnN3 (HMP Unified Metabolic Analysis Network 3) is the shotgun functional analysis de facto standard. Three-tiered search strategy:

  1. Tier 1 — MetaPhlAn4 species fingerprint: identify which species are present (clade-specific markers).
  2. Tier 2 — Bowtie2 against ChocoPhlAn pangenome: pull the pangenome for the detected species; bowtie2 reads. Usually > 60% reads match, and they retain species attribution.
  3. Tier 3 — translated DIAMOND against UniRef90: leftover reads are translated and aligned with DIAMOND. Captures rare species or novel genes.
  4. Three output tables: (1) genefamilies (UniRef90, stratifiable by species), (2) pathabundance (MetaCyc pathways), (3) pathcoverage.

Stratification is the killer feature: HUMAnN3 doesn't just say "butyrate synthesis is down" — it tells you "the drop is 30% from Roseburia, 40% from F. prausnitzii, 30% unclassified". Crucial for mechanistic clinical work.

QC reads→ MetaPhlAn4 species→ ChocoPhlAn pangenome→ UniRef90 DIAMOND→ KO / MetaCyc→ Differential

HUMAnN3 程式碼

# 1. 合併 paired-end (HUMAnN3 吃 single concat)
cat clean_R1.fq.gz clean_R2.fq.gz > sample1.fq.gz

# 2. HUMAnN3 主流程
humann --input sample1.fq.gz \
  --output humann_out/ \
  --threads 16 \
  --search-mode uniref90 \
  --metaphlan-options="--bowtie2db /db/metaphlan4" \
  --remove-temp-output

# 主要輸出：
#   sample1_genefamilies.tsv    UniRef90 family × sample, |species|
#   sample1_pathabundance.tsv   MetaCyc pathway abundance
#   sample1_pathcoverage.tsv    pathway 完整度 (0-1)

# 3. 合併多樣本
humann_join_tables -i humann_out/ -o all_gf.tsv --file_name genefamilies
humann_join_tables -i humann_out/ -o all_path.tsv --file_name pathabundance
# 4. 標準化為 CPM 或 relative abundance
humann_renorm_table -i all_gf.tsv -o all_gf.cpm.tsv --units cpm
humann_renorm_table -i all_path.tsv -o all_path.relab.tsv --units relab

# 5. Regroup UniRef90 → KO (KEGG Orthology)
humann_regroup_table -i all_gf.cpm.tsv \
  -g uniref90_ko \
  -o all_KO.cpm.tsv

# 加描述
humann_rename_table -i all_KO.cpm.tsv -n kegg-orthology -o all_KO.named.tsv

# 6. Regroup → GO / Pfam / eggNOG (其他映射)
humann_regroup_table -i all_gf.cpm.tsv -g uniref90_go -o all_GO.tsv
humann_regroup_table -i all_gf.cpm.tsv -g uniref90_pfam -o all_Pfam.tsv

# 7. 拆 stratified vs unstratified
humann_split_stratified_table -i all_path.relab.tsv -o split_out/
# split_out/all_path.relab_stratified.tsv      ← 帶物種來源
# split_out/all_path.relab_unstratified.tsv    ← 總和
# 8. 視覺化 — 帶物種貢獻的 barplot
humann_barplot --input all_path.relab_stratified.tsv \
  --focal-metadata disease \
  --last-metadata disease \
  --output butyrate_barplot.png \
  --focal-feature "PWY-5022: butyrate biosynthesis" \
  --sort sum

# 9. 用 R 對 KO 做差異分析 (compositional)
library(ALDEx2); library(Maaslin2)
ko <- read.table("all_KO.cpm.tsv", sep="\t", header=TRUE, row.names=1)
ko <- ko[!grepl("\\|", rownames(ko)),]  # keep unstratified only

# ALDEx2 對 KO
x <- aldex.clr(ko, conds=group, mc.samples=128)
res <- cbind(aldex.ttest(x), aldex.effect(x))

# MaAsLin2 帶協變量
Maaslin2(input_data=ko, input_metadata=meta,
         output="maaslin_KO", normalization="NONE",
         transform="LOG", analysis_method="LM",
         fixed_effects=c("disease","age","sex"),
         random_effects=c("subject"))
PICRUSt2

PICRUSt2 與 NSTI

PICRUSt2 (Phylogenetic Investigation of Communities by Reconstruction of Unobserved States 2) 的核心想法：

  1. 用所有已測序的細菌基因組建一棵參考系統發育樹，每個葉子標註其 KO / EC / pathway 含量。
  2. 對你的 ASV，用 SEPP 或 EPA-ng 插入到參考樹。
  3. 對該 ASV「祖先狀態重建 (hidden state prediction)」推估其 KO 含量。
  4. 乘以 ASV 豐度 → 樣本的 KO 豐度。

NSTI 是品質指標：NSTINearest Sequenced Taxon Index：你的 ASV 在參考樹上「最近已測序鄰居」的演化距離。0 = 完美匹配；2 = 很遠 (預測不可信)。 衡量 ASV 與最近參考的演化距離。預設過濾 NSTI > 2；嚴格研究用 NSTI > 0.5。

PICRUSt2 (Phylogenetic Investigation of Communities by Reconstruction of Unobserved States 2) core idea:

  1. Build a reference phylogenetic tree from all sequenced bacterial genomes; annotate each leaf with KO / EC / pathway counts.
  2. Place your ASVs into this tree via SEPP or EPA-ng.
  3. Apply ancestral state reconstruction (hidden state prediction) to estimate the ASV's KO content.
  4. Multiply by ASV abundance → per-sample KO abundance.

NSTI is the quality flag: NSTINearest Sequenced Taxon Index: phylogenetic distance from your ASV to its nearest sequenced neighbor on the reference tree. 0 = perfect match; 2 = far (unreliable). measures how far an ASV is from its nearest reference. Default filter NSTI > 2; for rigorous work, > 0.5.

NSTI = mean phylogenetic distance from query ASV to nearest reference genome (on tree) NSTI

代謝模型

代謝建模

當你有 MAG 或 isolate genome，可以建立 基因組規模代謝模型 (GEM)：把所有預測的代謝反應寫成矩陣，用 flux balance analysis (FBA) 預測穩態下代謝物流量。

  • CarveMe：從 genome 自動生成 GEM (15 分鐘/基因組)。
  • gapseq：另一個 GEM 自動生成，含 gap-filling。
  • MICOM：把社群多個 GEM 連動做 community-level FBA；預測群落代謝物產出。
  • COBRA Toolbox (MATLAB) / cobrapy (Python)：分析與優化 GEM。

With MAGs or isolate genomes, you can build a genome-scale metabolic model (GEM): encode all predicted reactions in a matrix and use flux balance analysis (FBA) to predict steady-state metabolite fluxes.

  • CarveMe: automatically generate GEMs from genomes (~15 min/genome).
  • gapseq: alternative GEM builder with gap-filling.
  • MICOM: couple multiple GEMs to do community FBA; predicts community-level metabolite outputs.
  • COBRA Toolbox (MATLAB) / cobrapy (Python): analyze and optimize GEMs.

MICOM 案例： 給定 IBD 病人糞便宏基因組 → 提取 MAG → 對每個 MAG 跑 CarveMe → 用 MICOM 模擬群落代謝 → 預測「丁酸生產量比健康低 5×」。再用糞便代謝體驗證——這就是 mechanistic loop。 Given IBD patient stool metagenome → extract MAGs → run CarveMe per MAG → run MICOM for community simulation → predict "butyrate production is 5× lower than healthy". Validate with stool metabolomics — that's the mechanistic loop.

案例：HMP2 IBD 多組學

Lloyd-Price J. et al. 2019, Nature 569:655-662

背景：132 位 IBD 受試者 (Crohn's / UC / non-IBD)，每位每 2 週採樣，共 1785 個糞便樣本。多組學包含：16S amplicon、shotgun metagenomics、metatranscriptomics、proteomics、metabolomics、virome、host transcriptomics。

方法：

  1. HUMAnN3 計算每樣本 ~10,000 個 KO 與 700 個 MetaCyc 路徑
  2. MaAsLin2 對 KO、pathway、代謝物、轉錄做差異分析，控制 age / sex / diagnosis / collection
  3. 橫向跨組學整合 (HAllA 找 cross-omics 關聯)

關鍵發現：

  • 「Dysbiosis state」是個自家定義的高度發炎時刻——不是穩態，而是交替進入。
  • 色胺酸代謝重組：indole 路徑 (健康) 被 kynurenine 路徑 (發炎) 取代。
  • SCFA 合成下降：丁酸合成相關基因下降，與糞便丁酸濃度線性相關。
  • 硫化氫產生上升：dissimilatory sulfate reduction (DSR) 路徑增強——可能直接損傷腸上皮。
  • Bile acid 改變：bsh (bile salt hydrolase) 與 baiCD (7α-dehydroxylation) 下降，未結合膽酸減少。
  • 單一物種無法解釋，但「功能簽名」橫跨整個 cohort 一致。

啟示：① 縱向設計重要 (穩態研究會錯失 dysbiosis 視窗)；② 多組學整合 >> 單一組學；③ 把功能當主訊號、物種當機制解釋；④ 公開資料 (ibdmdb.org) 仍持續被引用——可作為你的 reanalysis 基礎。

Background: 132 IBD subjects (Crohn's / UC / non-IBD), sampled every 2 weeks, 1785 stool samples total. Multi-omics: 16S amplicon, shotgun metagenomics, metatranscriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics, virome, host transcriptomics.

Methods:

  1. HUMAnN3 computed ~10,000 KOs and 700 MetaCyc pathways per sample
  2. MaAsLin2 differential analysis on KOs, pathways, metabolites, transcripts, controlling age/sex/diagnosis/site
  3. Cross-omics integration with HAllA

Key findings:

  • "Dysbiosis state" — a defined inflamed time-window; not steady, samples alternate in and out.
  • Tryptophan rerouting: the indole path (healthy) gives way to the kynurenine path (inflammation).
  • SCFA synthesis down: butyrate-synthesis genes drop, linearly correlated with stool butyrate levels.
  • Hydrogen sulfide up: dissimilatory sulfate reduction (DSR) is upregulated — may directly damage gut epithelium.
  • Bile acid changes: bsh (bile salt hydrolase) and baiCD (7α-dehydroxylation) drop, unconjugated bile acids shrink.
  • No single species explains, but the "functional signature" is reproducible across the cohort.

Take-home: (1) longitudinal design matters — cross-sectional misses the dysbiosis window; (2) multi-omics integration >> single layer; (3) function is the primary signal, species the mechanistic interpretation; (4) the public dataset (ibdmdb.org) is still actively reused — a great starting point for your own reanalysis.

互動模擬二

陷阱

5 個關鍵陷阱

① 預測當事實： PICRUSt2 是「推估」，準確度依賴參考樹涵蓋度。聲稱「IBD 患者丁酸合成路徑下降」只用 PICRUSt2 是不夠的。談機制必須 shotgun 驗證或代謝體量化。NSTI > 0.5 的研究尤其要謹慎。 PICRUSt2 is an inference dependent on reference tree coverage. Claiming "IBD patients lose butyrate synthesis pathways" from PICRUSt2 alone is insufficient. Mechanistic claims require shotgun verification or metabolomic quantification. NSTI > 0.5 studies should be especially careful.
② Raw counts 比較： 樣本間 library size 差異會干擾——10 Gb 樣本當然有更多 KO reads。對策：HUMAnN3 內建 --units cpm 或 --units relab；對 KO counts 做 CLR + linear model。 Library-size differences confound — a 10 Gb sample naturally has more KO reads. Use HUMAnN3's --units cpm or --units relab; apply CLR + linear model on KO counts.
③ 宿主干擾： 未過濾的 ~5% 人類 reads 會在 HUMAnN3 出錯「對應到細菌 KO」——尤其 ribosomal 與 translation KO 一定爆。對策：HUMAnN3 前 KneadData 對 human + rRNA 過濾。 Unfiltered ~5% human reads will misalign to bacterial KOs in HUMAnN3 — ribosomal and translation KOs especially balloon. Filter with KneadData against human + rRNA before HUMAnN3.
④ Enrichment 背景： 用 human gene-set enrichment 的方式對微生物 KO 跑 hypergeometric——背景集合錯！對策：用 MaAsLin2 / ALDEx2 直接對 KO；若做 enrichment，背景應為「樣本中偵測到的所有 KO」，非全 KEGG。 Running hypergeometric enrichment on microbial KOs with a human gene-set-style background is wrong! Use MaAsLin2 / ALDEx2 directly on KOs; if doing enrichment, the background must be "all KOs detected in your samples", not the whole KEGG.
⑤ Abundance vs Coverage： HUMAnN3 有兩個輸出：pathabundance (路徑酵素的總豐度) 與 pathcoverage (路徑酵素完整度，0-1)。abundance 高但 coverage 低 = 部分酵素過多但路徑沒拼齊。機制研究用 coverage，定量比較用 abundance。 HUMAnN3 outputs both pathabundance (total enzyme abundance) and pathcoverage (fraction of pathway's enzymes present, 0–1). High abundance + low coverage = some enzymes over-represented but pathway incomplete. Use coverage for mechanism, abundance for quantitative comparison.
