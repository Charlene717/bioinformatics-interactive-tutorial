Shotgun Metagenomics

從整個群落隨機定序——獲得 strain 級組成、功能基因清單，並重建可發表的 metagenome-assembled genomes。

Random sequencing of the whole community — get strain-level composition, the full functional gene catalog, and reconstruct publication-grade metagenome-assembled genomes.

🟡 Intermediate🔴 Advanced🟣 Biostatistician
為什麼重要

為什麼 Shotgun？

2019 年 Pasolli 等人 (Cell) 從 9428 個公開人體 metagenome 重建出 154,723 個 metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs)，其中 4,930 個是「人類腸道中從未培養過」的新菌——將已知人體微生物多樣性翻倍以上。這一切只用 Illumina 短讀 shotgun + 純運算實現，沒有任何培養。

shotgun 帶來的能力，16S 永遠做不到：

  • Strain-level 追蹤：辨識同一 E. coli 物種下的 K12 vs O157:H7；FMT 後新菌種是否成功定殖。
  • 抗生素抗藥基因組 (resistome)：直接量化 blaCTX-M、mecA、vanA 等，CARDComprehensive Antibiotic Resistance Database：含 3000+ 抗性基因、可用 RGI 工具掃 shotgun reads。、ResFinder 為標準資料庫。
  • 毒力因子 (virulence)：Shiga toxin、TTSS effector、毒蛋白譜——VFDB 資料庫。
  • 病毒體與噬菌體：CheckV、VirSorter、geNomad 偵測整合的前噬菌體 (prophage) 與裂解性病毒。
  • HGT (水平基因轉移)：metaCHIP、WAAFLE 偵測「同一基因在多個物種間穿梭」的事件。
  • 從讀到 MAG：重建從未培養的菌的完整基因組，提交到 NCBI 變為新參考序列。

In 2019, Pasolli et al. (Cell) reconstructed 154,723 metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs) from 9428 public human metagenomes — of which 4,930 represent species never cultured — more than doubling the known human gut microbial diversity. All from Illumina short-read shotgun and pure compute, with zero culture.

Capabilities shotgun unlocks that 16S can never offer:

  • Strain-level tracking: distinguish K12 vs O157:H7 within E. coli; track FMT engraftment.
  • Resistome: directly quantify blaCTX-M, mecA, vanA, etc.; standard refs are CARDComprehensive Antibiotic Resistance Database: 3000+ resistance genes; scan shotgun reads with RGI., ResFinder.
  • Virulence factors: Shiga toxin, TTSS effectors, full virulence panel — VFDB.
  • Virome & phages: CheckV, VirSorter, geNomad detect integrated prophages and lytic viruses.
  • Horizontal gene transfer (HGT): metaCHIP, WAAFLE detect "same gene shuffling among species".
  • Reads → MAG: reconstruct full genomes of never-cultured taxa, submit to NCBI as new references.
比喻： 想像你把 1000 本書全撕碎、扔進一個大袋子，再隨機抓出幾百萬張紙片。Shotgun metagenomics 就是這個袋子。Read-based 分類像翻找特定書名的紙片快速統計來源；Assembly + binning則是試圖重新組裝每本書——同一本書的紙片有相似的字體 (k-mer 組成)、用同樣的紙張 (覆蓋深度)，因此可被聚到一起。新書 (新菌) 就這樣被「發現」。 Imagine shredding 1000 books into one bag and picking out a few million paper scraps. That's shotgun metagenomics. Read-based classification = quickly count which books contributed each scrap. Assembly + binning = try to reassemble each book — scraps from the same book share font (k-mer composition) and paper stock (coverage), so they cluster. Brand-new books (novel species) are "discovered" this way.
兩大策略

Read-based vs Assembly-based

📖 Read-based

把每條 read 直接對照已知參考資料庫分類。常以 k-mer長度為 k 的核酸子序列。Kraken2 預設 k=35，把所有 ref genome 的 k-mer 編入索引，查 read 的 k-mer 反查物種。 為單位查表。

優：快 (Kraken2 8 GB/min)、設置簡單、低豐度物種偵測佳。

限：完全依賴 reference；新物種會被歸到最近祖先 (LCA) 或漏掉；無法看 strain (除 inStrain)。

工具：Kraken2、Bracken、MetaPhlAn4、Centrifuge、KrakenUniq、Sourmash。

Map each read directly to a known reference database. Lookup is typically by k-merA subsequence of length k. Kraken2 defaults to k=35: index all reference genome k-mers, look up each read's k-mers to assign taxonomy..

Pro: fast (Kraken2 = 8 GB/min), simple, good detection of low-abundance taxa.

Con: fully reference-dependent; novel taxa fall back to LCA or get missed; cannot see strain (unless inStrain).

Tools: Kraken2, Bracken, MetaPhlAn4, Centrifuge, KrakenUniq, Sourmash.

VS
🧩 Assembly-based

先 de novo 組裝 reads → contigscontiguous sequence：組裝後相連的長序列片段，通常數 kb 到 Mb。 → 分箱 → MAGMetagenome-Assembled Genome：用 binning 把屬於同一菌的 contigs 聚到一起所重建出的「準完整基因組」。。

優：偵測新物種、完整基因內容、可重建未培養菌的 reference genome。

限：慢 (100×+ CPU)、需 ≥ 10× 覆蓋的物種才組得起、低豐度菌會「碎」。

工具：metaSPAdes、MEGAHIT、MetaBAT2、CONCOCT、CheckM2、GTDB-Tk、dRep、Anvi'o。

De-novo assemble reads → contigsContiguous sequence: long stretches of joined-up DNA from assembly, typically a few kb to Mb. → bin → MAGMetagenome-Assembled Genome: the near-complete genome obtained when binning groups contigs of one organism..

Pro: detects novel taxa, full gene content, reconstructs reference genomes of never-cultured organisms.

Con: slow (100×+ CPU); only ≥10× coverage species assemble well; rare taxa fragment.

Tools: metaSPAdes, MEGAHIT, MetaBAT2, CONCOCT, CheckM2, GTDB-Tk, dRep, Anvi'o.

當代最佳實踐： 兩個都跑。Read-based 給快速概覽和低豐度菌；Assembly 給 strain、新菌與功能基因。HMP2 和 Pasolli 2019 都是「並行」設計。 Do both. Read-based for a quick overview + low-abundance detection; assembly for strain, novel taxa, and gene content. HMP2 and Pasolli 2019 both run them in parallel.
前處理

不可省略：前處理

① Adapter / quality trimming

fastp / Trimmomatic / cutadapt 移除 adapter、過低品質 reads。fastp 速度與報告最佳。

fastp / Trimmomatic / cutadapt remove adapters and low-quality reads. fastp wins on speed + reporting.

② 宿主 DNA 去除

用 Bowtie2 / KneadData 對人類基因組 (CHM13 v2) 比對，丟掉 mapped reads。糞便樣本 1-5%、口腔 30-60%、組織活檢 > 90% 是人類 DNA。不去除 = 你買的 shotgun 變成「人類重定序」。

Map to the human genome (CHM13 v2) with Bowtie2 / KneadData and discard mapped reads. Stool is 1-5%, oral 30-60%, tissue biopsies > 90% human DNA. Skip this = you paid for shotgun and got human resequencing.

③ 去除 PhiX

Illumina spike-in 對照。BBduk / BWA 對 PhiX 過濾。

Illumina spike-in control. Filter with BBduk / BWA against PhiX.

④ Negative controls

空 buffer 跑全流程；用 decontam / SCRuB 標識試劑污染物。對低生物量樣本必要。

Run blank buffer through full pipeline; flag kit contaminants with decontam / SCRuB. Mandatory for low-biomass samples.

Kraken2 演算法

Kraken2 + Bracken 原理

Kraken2 的核心：

  1. 建索引 (offline)：對每個參考基因組切 k-mer (預設 k=35)，建立「k-mer → 物種」對應表。同一 k-mer 出現在多個物種時，記錄為這些物種的 Lowest Common Ancestor (LCA)。
  2. 查詢 read：把 read 切成滑動 k-mer，每個 k-mer 查表，得到一串「途中 taxon」。沿樹找出最佳路徑賦予該 read 一個 taxon。
  3. 輸出：每條 read 的分類 + 樣本層級 report (kraken2.report)。

為何要 Bracken？Kraken2 把 read 卡在 LCA 節點時 (例：屬不上更深 species)，會「升級」到較高分類層級。Bracken 用 Bayesian 把這些 reads 重新分配回 species，獲得「實際 species 豐度」估計。

Kraken2 core:

  1. Index (offline): shred each reference genome into k-mers (default k=35); build a "k-mer → taxon" table. If a k-mer appears in multiple genomes, record the Lowest Common Ancestor (LCA).
  2. Query a read: slide k-mers across the read, look each up, get a sequence of "intermediate taxa". Climb the taxonomy tree and assign the read one taxon along the optimal path.
  3. Output: per-read classification + per-sample report.

Why Bracken? When Kraken2 hits an LCA node (the read cannot be resolved to species), it "promotes" the read to a higher rank. Bracken Bayesian-redistributes such reads back to species, giving a more honest species-level abundance.

P(species | reads at higher rank) ∝ P(reads | species) × P(species) Bracken Bayesian redistribution
比喻： Kraken2 像問人「你來自哪？」如果你的口音同時像日本、韓國、中國 (k-mer 在多個 genome 共享)，它回答「東亞」(LCA)。Bracken 像問了 1 萬人後，用統計把這些「東亞」的人按照族群實際比例分回日本、韓國、中國。 Kraken2 is like asking a person "where are you from?" If your accent matches Japan, Korea, and China (k-mers shared across genomes), it answers "East Asia" (LCA). Bracken polls 10,000 people, then statistically redistributes the "East Asian" answers into Japan / Korea / China by realistic proportions.

組裝原理

de Bruijn 與分箱

de Bruijn graph把所有 reads 切成 k-mer，每個 k-mer 為節點，重疊 k-1 鹼基為邊。組裝 = 找通過所有邊的路徑 (Eulerian path)。 是現代短讀組裝的基礎。對 metagenome 的挑戰：

  • 不均勻覆蓋：豐度 50% 的菌深 100×；豐度 0.1% 的菌深 0.5×。傳統假設「均勻覆蓋」失敗。
  • 重複序列跨物種：rRNA、insertion sequence、transposase 出現在多個基因組——graph 中產生「分支結點」。
  • strain 變異：同一物種不同 strain SNP 差幾百個 → graph 中產生「泡泡 (bubble)」。

解法：metaSPAdes 用多 k-mer (從 k=21 到 k=127) 漸進組裝、用 paired-end 訊息消除歧義；MEGAHIT 用 succinct de Bruijn graph 大幅省記憶體 (適合大樣本)。

組裝後得到 contigs：每個都可能來自任意一個菌。Binning 把它們聚回各自基因組：

de Bruijn graphShred reads into k-mers; each k-mer is a node, edges link nodes overlapping by k-1 bases. Assembly = find a path through all edges (Eulerian path). underpins modern short-read assembly. Metagenomic challenges:

  • Uneven coverage: a 50%-abundance microbe is 100× deep; a 0.1% microbe is 0.5×. Classic "uniform coverage" assumption breaks.
  • Cross-species repeats: rRNA, ISs, transposases appear in many genomes — branching nodes in the graph.
  • Strain variation: hundreds of SNPs between strains → "bubbles" in the graph.

Solutions: metaSPAdes uses progressive multi-k (k=21 → 127), paired-end info to resolve ambiguities; MEGAHIT uses a succinct de Bruijn graph saving huge memory (better for large samples).

After assembly you get contigs — each could belong to any microbe. Binning regroups them into genomes:

① 組成特徵 (TNF)

同一物種基因組 4-mer 頻率相似 (TNF 指紋)。不同 phylum TNF 距離大。

Genomes of one species share a 4-mer (tetranucleotide) frequency fingerprint. Different phyla have very different TNFs.

② 覆蓋深度

同一基因組的 contigs 應有相似覆蓋；跨多樣本時，相關性更強——豐度高處皆高、低處皆低。

Contigs of one genome should share coverage; across multiple samples, the correlation is even stronger — co-vary together.

跨樣本分箱： 如果你有 ≥ 10 個相關樣本，把每個樣本的 reads 都映回 co-assembly 的 contigs 算 coverage 向量。一個物種的所有 contigs 在 10 個樣本中應有類似的相對豐度模式——這比單樣本 TNF 強得多。MetaBAT2、CONCOCT、SemiBin2 都吃這種多樣本覆蓋。 If you have ≥10 related samples, map every sample's reads to the co-assembly contigs and compute coverage vectors. All contigs of one species should show a similar abundance pattern across the 10 samples — far stronger than single-sample TNF. MetaBAT2, CONCOCT, SemiBin2 all consume multi-sample coverage.

Rule of thumb: MAG reconstruction needs >10× coverage of that species. 1% abundance, 5 Mb genome, 10 Gb sample = ~20× → medium-high quality. Multi-sample co-assembly can drop this to ~3× (cross-sample signal is stronger).

案例研究：Pasolli 2019 — 154,723 MAGs

Pasolli E. et al. 2019, Cell 176:649-662

背景：2019 年前已知人類腸道菌約 2000 種，但 70% 的人體微生物從未培養過。Pasolli 等人重新分析所有公開 shotgun (9428 samples × 5 大洲 × 18 體表位)。

方法：

  1. 每個樣本獨立 metaSPAdes 組裝
  2. MetaBAT2 + CONCOCT + MaxBin2 → DAS_Tool 集成
  3. CheckM 過濾 (≥ 50% 完整度，< 5% 汙染)
  4. dRep 跨樣本去重 (95% ANI = 同 species)
  5. BLAST 對 NCBI 找出「從未報告過」的 4930 個物種

關鍵發現：

  • 新 species 中 77% 屬 Firmicutes、77% 不能在公司資料庫找到。
  • 非西方人 (撒哈拉、東南亞、中南美) 樣本貢獻最多新菌——印證「西方化飲食縮小微生物多樣性」假說。
  • 許多新菌帶有獨特代謝功能 (例如 secondary bile acid 代謝)。

啟示：① shotgun 公開資料是個金礦——重新分析可發大論文；② non-Western 樣本最有價值；③ MAGs 已經是現代微生物基因組學的基礎建設。

Background: Before 2019 the human gut was thought to contain ~2000 species, yet 70% of human microbes had never been cultured. Pasolli et al. reanalyzed all public shotgun data (9428 samples × 5 continents × 18 body sites).

Methods:

  1. Per-sample metaSPAdes assembly
  2. MetaBAT2 + CONCOCT + MaxBin2 → DAS_Tool ensemble
  3. CheckM filter (≥ 50% completeness, < 5% contamination)
  4. dRep cross-sample dereplication (95% ANI = one species)
  5. BLAST vs NCBI to flag "never reported" species — 4,930 of them

Key findings:

  • 77% of novel species belonged to Firmicutes; 77% had no match in any company database.
  • Non-Western cohorts (sub-Saharan Africa, SE Asia, Latin America) contributed the most novel species — supporting the "westernization shrinks diversity" hypothesis.
  • Many novel species carry unique metabolic functions (e.g., secondary bile acid metabolism).

Lessons: (1) Public shotgun data is a goldmine — reanalysis can drive big papers; (2) non-Western samples are most valuable; (3) MAGs are now the infrastructure of modern microbial genomics.

陷阱

5 個關鍵陷阱

① 宿主 DNA： 糞便 1-10%、口腔 30-60%、組織 > 90% 是人類 DNA。不去除會：浪費你 80% 的計算、Kraken2 把人類 reads 錯分到 Homo 旁的 outlier、HUMAnN3 結果全部失真。對策：KneadData + CHM13 v2。 Stool is 1-10%, oral 30-60%, tissue >90% human DNA. Skipping removal: wastes 80% of your compute, Kraken2 mis-assigns human reads to Homo-neighboring taxa, HUMAnN3 results become noise. Fix: KneadData + CHM13 v2.
② Kraken2 假陽性： Kraken2 預設 confidence=0 會把每條 read 強制分類，導致 1000+ species 報告，其中大半是假陽性。對策：① 用 KrakenUniq 計獨特 k-mer 數過濾；② 設 confidence ≥ 0.1；③ 用 Bracken 後篩 ≥ 100 reads。 Kraken2 default confidence=0 force-classifies every read, reporting 1000+ species — most false. Fix: (1) KrakenUniq's unique-k-mer count filter; (2) set confidence ≥0.1; (3) Bracken post-filter ≥100 reads.
③ Chimeric MAG： 兩個近親物種的 contigs 被 binning 混在一起。CheckM2 看起來完整度高，但實際上「拼錯」。對策：① 跑 GUNC (Genome UNClutter)、② Anvi'o 視覺檢查、③ 看到 SNP 密度異常變動就懷疑。 Contigs from two close relatives get binned together. CheckM2 reports high completeness but the bin is hybrid. Fix: (1) run GUNC (Genome UNClutter); (2) Anvi'o visual inspection; (3) suspect when SNP density fluctuates abnormally.
④ 低豐度組裝： 豐度 < 0.5% 的菌單一樣本通常 < 5× 覆蓋，組不出來。對策：① co-assembly (但 RAM 上限)、② 富集 (mini-Bulk)、③ 接受「找不到所有菌」。 Taxa < 0.5% are usually < 5× covered in single samples — won't assemble. Fix: (1) co-assembly (RAM-bound); (2) enrich (mini-bulk); (3) accept you can't reconstruct everything.
⑤ Raw counts 比較： 不同樣本 library size 差異大 (1 Gb vs 10 Gb)，raw counts 不可直接比。對策：HUMAnN3 內建 normalize 為 CPM/relab；或對 KO counts 做 CLR + linear model。 Library sizes differ 10× (1 Gb vs 10 Gb); raw counts cannot be compared directly. Fix: HUMAnN3 normalizes to CPM/relab built-in; or CLR + linear model on KO counts.
