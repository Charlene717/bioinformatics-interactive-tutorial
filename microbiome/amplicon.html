16S / ITS Amplicon

最普及的微生物群體調查——用一段保守 + 可變交替的 ribosomal RNA 基因當「條碼」識別細菌或真菌。

The most popular microbiome survey — use a conserved+variable ribosomal RNA gene as a "barcode" to identify bacteria or fungi.

🟢 Beginner🟡 Intermediate🟣 Biostatistician
為什麼重要

從一管糞便到一個診斷

1980 年代以前，要知道一個糞便樣本裡有什麼細菌，唯一方法是培養 (culture)——但 70-90% 的腸道菌不能在實驗室培養。微生物學家當時稱這種困境為「great plate count anomaly」(培養盤計數悖論)：顯微鏡下看到 1000 個細菌，培養盤只長出 10 個。

1985 年 Carl Woese 用 16S rRNA 序列重畫了生命之樹；1990s Norman Pace 推廣「不培養也能分類」的環境 PCR；2010 年 Caporaso 等人發表 EMP 16S 標準流程，從此一管糞便 → 一個 fastq → 一個物種清單只需幾天。

實際應用例子：

  • 新生兒 NICU：早產兒糞便 16S 可預測壞死性小腸結腸炎 (NEC) 風險，Klebsiella 與 Clostridium 過度繁殖是警訊。
  • 口腔 16S 屏障：牙周炎患者 Porphyromonas gingivalis 、Tannerella、Treponema 三聯體 (red complex) 比例上升。
  • 陰道菌相：Lactobacillus dominance vs 細菌性陰道炎 (BV) 的 community state types I–V，被臨床用來追蹤健康狀態。
  • 農業：作物根際 16S 預測抗病性，已被當作種植決策依據。

Before the 1980s, the only way to know what bacteria were in a stool sample was culture — but 70–90% of gut microbes cannot be grown in the lab. Microbiologists called this the "great plate count anomaly": 1000 bacteria under the microscope, only 10 colonies on the plate.

In 1985 Carl Woese used 16S rRNA sequences to redraw the tree of life; in the 1990s Norman Pace popularized "culture-independent" environmental PCR; in 2010 Caporaso et al. published the EMP 16S protocol. Today one stool tube → one fastq → one species list takes days.

Real-world examples:

  • Neonatal ICU: stool 16S predicts necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) risk; Klebsiella and Clostridium blooms are red flags.
  • Oral health: periodontitis patients show elevated "red complex" P. gingivalis/Tannerella/Treponema.
  • Vaginal microbiota: Lactobacillus dominance vs bacterial vaginosis are summarized into Community State Types I–V — used clinically to monitor reproductive health.
  • Agriculture: rhizosphere 16S predicts disease resistance and now informs planting decisions.
比喻： 16S rRNA 像每本書的ISBN 條碼——大家都有、長度標準、含有出版商與分類資訊。9 個可變區 (V1-V9) 像條碼裡的 9 個欄位；保守區是欄位之間的固定間隔，讓 PCR primer 能準確「抓住」。整段 1500 bp 像條碼上的所有數字，但 Illumina 短讀通常只能讀 V3-V4 (~460 bp) 兩個欄位——夠到 genus，但無法到 species。 16S rRNA is like the ISBN barcode on every book — universal, fixed-length, encoding publisher and category. The 9 variable regions (V1-V9) are like the 9 numeric fields; conserved regions are the spacers that let PCR primers "grip". The full 1500 bp is the whole barcode, but Illumina short reads usually capture only V3-V4 (~460 bp) — enough for genus, rarely species.
生物學基礎

為什麼是 16S？

核醣體 RNA (ribosomal RNA構成核醣體 (蛋白質合成工廠) 的 RNA。原核 70S 核醣體含 23S+5S (大次單位) 與 16S (小次單位)。真核 80S 含 28S+5.8S+5S 與 18S。) 基因有三個讓它成為「分類條碼」的理由：

  1. 普遍存在：每個細胞都有 (拷貝數 1–15)；古菌也用 16S。
  2. 速率分區：核糖體催化中心 (保守區) 演化極慢；外圍 stem-loop (可變區) 演化中速——剛好夠把 phylum 分開又能看到 genus 差異。
  3. 大量參考資料：SILVA、GreenGenes、RDP 收錄百萬條已分類序列。

區段對應：

The ribosomal RNA (ribosomal RNARNA that builds the ribosome (the protein synthesis factory). Prokaryotic 70S ribosome contains 23S+5S (large) and 16S (small). Eukaryotic 80S contains 28S+5.8S+5S and 18S.) gene wins as a taxonomic barcode for three reasons:

  1. Universal: every cell has it (copy number 1–15); archaea also use 16S.
  2. Rate compartmentalization: the catalytic core (conserved regions) evolves very slowly; peripheral stem-loops (variable regions) evolve at moderate rates — perfect to split phyla while still seeing genus differences.
  3. Massive reference databases: SILVA, GreenGenes, RDP host millions of classified sequences.

如何選 V 區： 先決定（1）你要研究的環境(腸 / 皮膚 / 海洋)、（2）感興趣的目標菌、（3）平台讀長。口訣：腸道 → V3-V4；皮膚 → V1-V3；海洋 → V4 (EMP)；真菌 → ITS2；想要 species → PacBio 全長 16S。 Decide (1) habitat (gut / skin / ocean), (2) target taxa, (3) platform read length. Rule of thumb: gut → V3-V4; skin → V1-V3; ocean → V4 (EMP); fungi → ITS2; species-level → PacBio full-length 16S.
ASV vs OTU

ASV vs OTU

2010 年代主流是把所有相似度 ≥ 97% 的序列「聚類」成一個 OTUOperational Taxonomic Unit：操作分類單位。傳統做法是把 PCR/定序錯誤「容忍」進去——任何相差 ≤ 3% 的序列視為同一 OTU。。這是個務實妥協：當時定序錯誤率高，聚類能掩蓋錯誤。但 OTU 邊界不穩定 (不同樣本聚出不同的代表序列)、跨研究無法直接比較。

2016 年 Callahan 等人 (DADA2) 與 Amir 等人 (Deblur) 推出去噪模型：用機率推論把序列「校正回真實」。每個唯一序列 = 一個 ASVAmplicon Sequence Variant：擴增子序列變異。每個 ASV 是一條「真實存在」的序列；理論上單鹼基差異也分為兩個 ASV。。ASV 的好處是跨研究可直接比對——相同 ASV 序列就是同一條 DNA。

The 2010s standard was to cluster sequences ≥ 97% similarity into one OTUOperational Taxonomic Unit. Traditional approach that "absorbs" PCR/sequencing errors — any sequences within 3% of each other are treated as one OTU.. A pragmatic compromise: error rates were high, clustering hid them. But OTU boundaries are unstable (different samples yield different representative sequences) and cross-study comparison fails.

In 2016 Callahan et al. (DADA2) and Amir et al. (Deblur) introduced denoising models: probabilistic inference that "corrects errors back to truth." Every unique sequence = one ASVAmplicon Sequence Variant. Each ASV is a "real" sequence; theoretically even a single-base difference splits two ASVs.. Big advantage: ASVs are directly comparable across studies — same ASV sequence is literally the same DNA.

  • 表 ID 隨機 (OTU_001 在另一研究無意義)
  • 記憶體 / 計算成本低
  • 舊資料、低品質定序仍用
  • Error-tolerant but low resolution
  • IDs are arbitrary (OTU_001 means nothing across studies)
  • Cheap in memory / compute
  • Still useful for legacy or low-quality data

Field consensus (Callahan et al. 2017, ISME J): use ASVs for new analyses. OTUs persist only for legacy data, ultra-low resources, or when you specifically need to absorb a known error mode (e.g., older PacBio CCS-2).
DADA2 機制

DADA2 的去噪原理

DADA2 (Divisive Amplicon Denoising Algorithm 2) 的核心想法：

  1. 學習 run-specific 錯誤模型：對每一個 Illumina lane (run)，估計 16 種 base transition 機率 (A→C, A→G, ... 含 quality score 條件)。同一儀器、不同 run 錯誤率不同。
  2. 「真實序列 + 錯誤」假設：假設 reads 是少數真實序列加上「依照學到的錯誤分佈」變異而來。對每個唯一序列計算「它真的存在」的後驗機率。
  3. 分割演算法：從最豐富序列開始，把較稀有序列分類為「衍生自真序列的錯誤」或「自己也是真序列」。重複到穩定。
  4. 結果：每個樣本得到一張「ASV × 樣本」計數表。

DADA2 (Divisive Amplicon Denoising Algorithm 2) core idea:

  1. Learn a run-specific error model: per Illumina lane, estimate 16 base-transition probabilities (A→C, A→G, …) conditioned on quality score. Same instrument, different run = different error rates.
  2. "True sequence + error" model: assume reads are derived from a few true sequences plus errors drawn from the learned distribution. Compute posterior probability each unique sequence is genuinely present.
  3. Divisive partitioning: start from the most abundant sequence; classify rarer sequences as "errors from a true one" or "novel true sequences". Iterate to convergence.
  4. Output: ASV × sample count table.
P(read | true_seq) = ∏i=1..L P( baseiread | baseitrue, Qi ) DADA2 錯誤模型
比喻： 想像一台印刷機印同一頁書 1000 次，但有 1% 的字會印錯。DADA2 假設「真實版本」只有幾個，先猜哪個字組合最可能；然後看每個觀察到的「不同版本」是「真的不同書」還是「印壞了的同一本」。Quality score 像是「這個字模糊度」，印刷越模糊 (Q score 越低) 越可能是錯字。 Imagine a press printing one page 1000 times, with 1% per-character error. DADA2 supposes there are only a few "real versions"; it first guesses the most likely true text, then asks whether each variant is "a different book" or "a misprint of the same one". Quality score plays the role of "how blurry that character looks" — lower Q = more likely an error.

path <- "raw_fastqs/"
fnF <- sort(list.files(path, pattern = "_R1.fastq.gz", full.names=TRUE))

# 0. 視覺化品質 (先看再決定 truncLen!)

# 1. 品質過濾
filtF <- file.path("filt", basename(fnF))
filtR <- file.path("filt", basename(fnR))
filterAndTrim(fnF, filtF, fnR, filtR,
              truncLen   = c(240, 200),   # 看品質圖決定
              maxEE      = c(2, 2),       # expected errors 上限
              truncQ     = 2,             # 切到第一個 Q<=2
              rm.phix    = TRUE, multithread = TRUE)

# 2. 學 run-specific 錯誤模型
errF <- learnErrors(filtF, multithread = TRUE, nbases = 1e8)
errR <- learnErrors(filtR, multithread = TRUE, nbases = 1e8)
plotErrors(errF, nominalQ = TRUE)  # 黑點應沿黑線

# 3. 主流程 (denoising → ASV)
ddF  <- dada(filtF, err = errF, multithread = TRUE, pool = "pseudo")
ddR  <- dada(filtR, err = errR, multithread = TRUE, pool = "pseudo")

案例研究：HMP 18 個部位的「指紋」

Huttenhower et al. 2012, Nature 486:207

HMP1 (2012) 對 242 位健康成人、18 個身體部位 (口腔多點、皮膚、鼻腔、糞便、陰道、唾液) 跑 V3-V5 16S。發現：

  • 每個部位都有「特徵社群」：糞便 Bacteroidetes + Firmicutes 主導；陰道 Lactobacillus 一枝獨秀 (低 alpha)；唾液 Streptococcus + Prevotella；皮膚 Cutibacterium。
  • 個體差異大、但部位特徵恆定：你的糞便與我的糞便差異，遠小於你的糞便與你的皮膚。
  • 陰道 alpha 最低 (Shannon ~0.7)、糞便最高 (~3.5)。健康陰道 = 單一 Lactobacillus 主導，這是「低多樣性 = 健康」的反直覺特例。
  • 功能比物種更穩定：個體間 KEGG pathway profile 相似度 > 物種 profile。

啟示：分析時把 body site 作為主要分組變量，不要把口腔和糞便混在一起。任何結論都應該分部位解讀。

HMP1 (2012) profiled 242 healthy adults at 18 body sites (multiple oral sites, skin, nares, stool, vagina, saliva) with V3-V5 16S. Findings:

  • Each site has a signature community: stool dominated by Bacteroidetes + Firmicutes; vagina dominated by Lactobacillus (low alpha); saliva by Streptococcus + Prevotella; skin by Cutibacterium.
  • Large between-subject variation, but body-site identity is preserved: your stool vs my stool diverges far less than your stool vs your skin.
  • Vagina has the lowest alpha (Shannon ~0.7), stool the highest (~3.5). Healthy vagina = single Lactobacillus dominance, a counterintuitive case where "low diversity = healthy."
  • Function more stable than taxonomy: KEGG pathway profiles are more similar across subjects than species profiles.

Take-home: include body site as the primary grouping variable; never pool oral and stool. Always interpret findings per body site.

陷阱

5 個關鍵陷阱

① 不要 Rarefy： 傳統做法是隨機 subsample 到固定深度。McMurdie & Holmes 2014 證明這浪費資料且增加變異。對策：raw count + ALDEx2 / ANCOM-BC，或 CLR + 線性模型。Rarefaction 唯一仍適用是計算 UniFrac 距離以保證可比。 Traditional practice was random sub-sampling to a fixed depth. McMurdie & Holmes 2014 showed this wastes data and inflates variance. Fix: use raw counts + ALDEx2/ANCOM-BC, or CLR + linear models. Rarefaction lingers only when computing UniFrac to ensure comparability.
② 拷貝數偏差： E. coli 有 7 個 16S 基因拷貝，Bifidobacterium 只有 1-3 個。直接讀 reads 數會「放大」高拷貝物種。對策：rrnDB 或 PICRUSt2 內建拷貝數校正；或承認此偏差只比較 case/control 內相同物種。 E. coli carries 7 copies of 16S; Bifidobacterium has 1-3. Raw read counts inflate high-copy species. Correct with rrnDB or PICRUSt2's built-in normalization, or compare same species across case/control only.
③ PCA vs PCoA： 在原始 OTU 計數上跑 PCA 是錯的——資料是 compositional，PCA 假設歐氏距離不成立。對策：用 PCoA (Bray-Curtis / UniFrac) 或 CLR + PCA。 Running PCA on raw OTU counts is wrong — data are compositional and PCA's Euclidean-distance assumption is invalid. Use PCoA (Bray-Curtis / UniFrac) or CLR + PCA.
④ Alpha 比較陷阱： Alpha 指數對 library size 敏感。Shannon 比 richness 穩。對策：在比較前 rarefy 到相同深度 (僅供 alpha)、或回歸殘差於 library size。 Alpha indices are sensitive to library size. Shannon is more robust than richness. Before comparing alpha across samples, rarefy to a common depth (alpha only), or regress out library size.
⑤ Chimera： PCR 過程中模板 A 的 partial product 跳到模板 B 上延伸，產生「不存在的雜合序列」。DADA2 內建 consensus 去 chimera 通常足夠；mothur 可額外 vsearch UCHIME。檢查 chimera 比例 > 25% 表示 PCR 條件有問題。 During PCR, a partial extension from template A jumps to template B, creating a hybrid that doesn't exist. DADA2's built-in consensus removal is usually enough; mothur can add vsearch UCHIME. Chimera fraction > 25% indicates poor PCR conditions.

PCoA 是線性 (eigendecomposition)，保留距離；NMDS 是非線性，保留秩序。樣本多時 PCoA 較快；NMDS 對非歐距離效果好。建議兩個都跑，看結論一不一致。 PCoA is linear (eigendecomposition), preserves distances; NMDS is non-linear, preserves ranks. PCoA is faster on large samples; NMDS handles non-Euclidean distances better. Run both and check consistency.
Unweighted：只看物種有無，對稀有菌敏感、適合演化生態問題。Weighted：考慮豐度，對優勢菌敏感。兩個都跑，常能互補揭露不同訊號。 Unweighted: presence/absence only; sensitive to rare taxa; great for evolutionary ecology. Weighted: incorporates abundance; sensitive to dominant taxa. Run both — they often reveal complementary signal.

Include one per run as a positive control. Quantifies (1) extraction bias, (2) primer coverage, (3) classifier accuracy. Strongly recommended ≥1 per batch.
