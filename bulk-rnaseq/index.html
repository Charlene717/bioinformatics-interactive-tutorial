開場故事

為什麼 Bulk RNA-seq 仍是 2025 年的王者？

單細胞 RNA-seq、空間轉錄組看起來更炫，但 Bulk RNA-seq 在 2024 年仍佔所有公開 RNA-seq 樣本的 70%+。原因？三個現實：

  1. 成本：每樣本 ~$100；單細胞 ~$1,500；空間 ~$2,500
  2. 統計力：對 cohort-level DE 分析（n≥10），bulk 的 power 仍勝過 pseudo-bulk
  3. 已知 baseline：20 年資料庫（GTEx、TCGA、ENCODE）都是 bulk，新研究若要與歷史 cohort 比對，bulk 是橋樑

掌握 Bulk RNA-seq 流程，等於掌握了體學分析的「通用語」——從這裡進階到 scRNA-seq、spatial、long-read RNA，會發現核心統計與工具邏輯都是延伸自 bulk。

Single-cell and spatial transcriptomics look glamorous, but bulk RNA-seq still accounts for 70%+ of all public RNA-seq samples in 2024. Why? Three pragmatic reasons:

  1. Cost: ~$100/sample (bulk) vs ~$1,500 (scRNA) vs ~$2,500 (spatial)
  2. Statistical power: for cohort DE (n≥10), bulk outperforms pseudo-bulk single-cell
  3. Established baseline: 20 years of data (GTEx, TCGA, ENCODE) are bulk — your new study connects to history through bulk

Master bulk RNA-seq and you master the lingua franca of omics. Progressing to scRNA / spatial / long-read RNA, you'll find the core statistics and tools are extensions of bulk.

總覽

完整分析流程

FASTQ→ QC / Trim→ Align→ Count→ Normalize→ DE→ GSEA

Bulk RNA-seq 量化樣本中所有細胞混合的 mRNA 表達量。雖然解析度比 scRNA-seq 粗，但深度更高、噪音更低，是研究兩組樣本（如 case vs. control）轉錄組差異最普及的方法。每章節對應一個關鍵流程步驟，建議按順序學習。

Bulk RNA-seq quantifies the averaged mRNA expression across all cells in a sample. Coarser than scRNA-seq but with greater depth and lower noise — the most popular way to compare transcriptomes between groups (case vs control). Each chapter targets a pipeline step; recommended to study in order.

類比：Bulk RNA-seq 就像「把一個城市所有人的飲食習慣平均成一個值」——你看不出富人吃魚子醬、窮人吃泡麵，但能告訴你城市整體的鹽攝取趨勢。在大量樣本下，這種「平均化」反而是統計力的優勢。
Analogy: Bulk RNA-seq is like averaging the dietary habits of an entire city into one number — you lose the rich-vs-poor contrast, but you accurately capture the city's overall salt intake trend. Across many cohorts, this averaging is a statistical strength.

生物學脈絡

從 mRNA 到 DEGs：一個分子的旅程

① 細胞內：mRNA 轉錄

細胞核中，DNA 被 RNA polymerase II 轉錄成 mRNA。每個基因每分鐘可能產生 1-100 份 mRNA，取決於 promoter 強度、TF 結合、染色質可及性。RNA-seq 試圖捕捉這一刻的「轉錄活動快照」——但別忘記 mRNA 半衰期（分鐘到小時不等），所以你看到的是過去 1-2 小時的平均，不是當下。

In the nucleus, DNA is transcribed by RNA polymerase II into mRNA. Each gene may produce 1–100 mRNAs per minute depending on promoter strength, TF binding, and chromatin accessibility. RNA-seq tries to capture a snapshot of transcriptional activity — but mRNA half-lives range minutes to hours, so you're seeing an average over the past 1–2 hours.

② 抽取：RNA 萃取與品質

從組織/細胞抽出 total RNA（含 mRNA、rRNA、tRNA、miRNA…）。RINRNA Integrity Number。Bioanalyzer 跑 28S/18S rRNA 比例自動判斷。10 = 完美完整、1 = 嚴重降解。RIN ≥ 7 是 RNA-seq 標準門檻。 ≥ 7 才適合 RNA-seq。rRNA 佔 total RNA 90%+，所以下一步要去 rRNA。

Total RNA is extracted (mRNA + rRNA + tRNA + miRNA…). RINRNA Integrity Number. Bioanalyzer-derived from 28S/18S rRNA ratio. 10 = perfect; 1 = severely degraded. RIN ≥ 7 is the RNA-seq standard. ≥ 7 is required for RNA-seq. Since rRNA is >90% of total RNA, it must be depleted next.

③ Library prep：兩種路徑

polyA selection（捕獲 mRNA 的 3' polyA 尾）：用 oligo-dT 磁珠。簡單便宜，但對降解 RNA 偏向 3'。
ribozero / rRNA depletion：用探針移除 rRNA。保留 non-polyA RNA（lncRNA、histone mRNA），對降解樣本更穩定。
選擇影響後續 QC 中 rRNA% 解讀，也影響 GSEA 的 gene-set 涵蓋。

polyA selection (capture mRNA 3' polyA tails) — uses oligo-dT beads. Simple, cheap, but biased toward 3' for degraded RNA.
ribozero / rRNA depletion — probes remove rRNA. Retains non-polyA RNA (lncRNA, histone mRNA), more stable for degraded samples.
This choice affects downstream rRNA% interpretation and GSEA gene-set coverage.

④ 反轉錄 + 片段化

mRNA → cDNA（反轉錄）→ 切成 200-500 bp 片段（fragmentation）。隨後加接頭、PCR 擴增。random hexamer priming 用六鹼基隨機引子，每個 6-mer 起始效率略不同——這就是 FastQC「per base sequence content」前 10-12 bp 偏差的根源（很多人誤以為是品質問題）。

mRNA → cDNA (reverse transcription) → fragmented to 200–500 bp → adapters ligated → PCR amplified. Random hexamer priming uses 6-base random primers with slightly different efficiencies — this is the root of FastQC's "per base sequence content" imbalance in the first 10–12 bp (often misread as a quality issue).

⑤ 定序 → FASTQ

Illumina SBS（synthesis-by-sequencing）每循環加入一個螢光標記 nucleotide，影像化解讀。輸出 FASTQ：每 read 4 行（ID、序列、+、品質）。從這裡開始，QC 與分析是分析師的責任。

Illumina SBS (synthesis-by-sequencing) adds one fluorescent nucleotide per cycle, imaging the result. Output: FASTQ — 4 lines per read (ID, sequence, +, quality). From here on, QC and analysis are the analyst's responsibility.

資料準備

範例資料集

學習 Bulk RNA-seq 最棒的公開資料集：

  • airway（Bioconductor）真實資料：人類氣管平滑肌細胞 ± dexamethasone，DESeq2 教學的招牌資料
  • pasilla（Bioconductor）真實資料：果蠅 splicing factor 敲低 vs. control
  • GTEx：53 種人類組織的大規模 RNA-seq，免費下載 count matrix
  • TCGA：33 種癌症的 paired tumor/normal 表達矩陣，含臨床資訊
  • recount3：超過 80 萬個 SRA 樣本統一處理後的 count matrix
  • ARCHS4：50 萬個 GEO RNA-seq 樣本的標準化矩陣，可一鍵 GO/KEGG enrichment

Top public datasets for learning Bulk RNA-seq:

  • airway (Bioconductor) Real Data: Human airway smooth muscle ± dexamethasone — DESeq2's signature teaching dataset
  • pasilla (Bioconductor) Real Data: Drosophila splicing-factor knockdown vs. control
  • GTEx: large-scale RNA-seq across 53 human tissues; free count matrices
  • TCGA: paired tumor/normal expression for 33 cancer types with clinical info
  • recount3: 800k+ SRA samples uniformly reprocessed into count matrices
  • ARCHS4: 500k GEO RNA-seq samples in a standardized matrix; one-click GO/KEGG enrichment
