完整分析流程

Bulk RNA-seq 量化一個樣本中所有細胞混合的 mRNA 表達量。雖然解析度比 scRNA-seq 粗，但深度更高、噪音更低，是研究兩組樣本（如 case vs. control）轉錄組差異最普及的方法。完整流程通常包含七個步驟：

Bulk RNA-seq quantifies the averaged mRNA expression across all cells in a sample. While coarser than scRNA-seq, it offers higher depth and lower noise — the most popular way to compare transcriptomes between groups (case vs. control). A full workflow has seven steps:

FASTQ→ QC / Trim→ Align→ Count→ Normalize→ DE→ GSEA

範例資料集

學習 Bulk RNA-seq 最棒的公開資料集：

  • airway（Bioconductor）：人類氣管平滑肌細胞 ± dexamethasone，DESeq2 教學的招牌資料。
  • pasilla（Bioconductor）：果蠅 splicing factor 敲低 vs. control。
  • GTEx：53 種人類組織的大規模 RNA-seq。
  • TCGA：33 種癌症的 paired tumor/normal 表達矩陣。
  • recount3：超過 80 萬個 SRA 樣本統一處理後的 count matrix。

Top public datasets for learning Bulk RNA-seq:

  • airway (Bioconductor): Human airway smooth muscle ± dexamethasone — DESeq2's signature teaching dataset.
  • pasilla (Bioconductor): Drosophila splicing-factor knockdown vs. control.
  • GTEx: Large-scale RNA-seq across 53 human tissues.
  • TCGA: Paired tumor/normal expression for 33 cancer types.
  • recount3: Over 800k SRA samples uniformly reprocessed into count matrices.
