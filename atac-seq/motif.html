Motif 與 Footprinting

🟡 Intermediate🔴 Advanced🟣 Expert
為何重要

從開放圖譜到 TF 動力學

ATAC peaks 只告訴你「這裡是 CRE」，但沒回答「是哪個 TF 在驅動這個 CRE？」。要回答這個問題，需要兩層分析：

  1. Motif Enrichment在 peaks 內掃描已知 TF 的 DNA 序列模式，統計是否比背景富集。：你的 peaks 是否富集某 TF 的 DNA 序列模式？答的是「潛在 結合」。
  2. Footprinting分析 motif 中心 Tn5 cut 訊號是否出現「凹陷」——TF 結合時擋住 Tn5。：motif 中心是否有 Tn5 cut signal 的凹陷？答的是「實際 結合」。

生物學意義：找出 master TFs，能解釋細胞身分、發育階段轉換、疾病異常表觀調控、藥物響應等。Footprint 的差異還能反映 TF 結合度量化動力學變化。

ATAC peaks tell you "here's a CRE" but not "which TF drives it?" Answering requires two layers:

  1. Motif EnrichmentScan peaks for known TF DNA patterns; test enrichment vs background.: Are peaks enriched for a TF's DNA pattern? Answers potential binding.
  2. FootprintingTest for a "dip" in Tn5 cut signal at motif centre — a bound TF blocks Tn5.: Does the motif centre show a dip in Tn5 cut signal? Answers actual binding.

Biology: Identify master TFs explaining cell identity, developmental transitions, disease epigenetic dysregulation, drug response. Footprint differentials quantify binding dynamics.

motif 是什麼

什麼是 motif？

當你把一個 TF（例如 GATA1）在基因組上所有已驗證的結合位點對齊，會發現它們的 DNA 序列雖然不完全相同，但在某些位置強烈偏好某種鹼基：

Align all verified binding sites of one TF (e.g., GATA1) and you'll find the sequences aren't identical but show strong base preferences at certain positions:

GATA motif 對齊 Pos: 1 2 3 4 5 6 site 1: A G A T A A site 2: A G A T A G site 3: T G A T A A site 4: A G A T A A site 5: A G A T A G ── ── ── ── ── ── → A G A T A R ← consensus (R = A 或 G) 這就是 motif：位置特異的鹼基偏好

把每個位置的鹼基頻率整理成矩陣，就是 PWMPosition Weight Matrix — 描述 motif 每位置 A/C/G/T 偏好的對數機率矩陣，是 motif 的數學表示。（Position Weight Matrix）；視覺化為 motif logo——字母高度 = 該位置 information content (bits)，最大 2 bits（完全固定）。

Tabulating base frequencies per position gives a PWMPosition Weight Matrix — log-probability matrix describing per-position base preferences; the mathematical motif. (Position Weight Matrix); visualised as a motif logo — letter height = information content (bits), max 2 (fully fixed).

PWM / Information Content PWMi,b = log2( fi,b / pb )    (f = frequency at pos i for base b; p = background freq) ICi = 2 − Hi = 2 + Σb fi,b · log2(fi,b) Information content
比喻： PWM 像「鎖芯」的形狀描述——某些位置必須是特定形狀（G 在 position 2），某些位置可以是任一鹽基（位置 6 是 A 或 G 都可）。TF 是「鑰匙」——只有形狀大致符合的鑰匙才能插進去。motif scanner（如 FIMO）就是把每段 DNA 試著當「鑰匙」插進「鎖芯」(PWM)，計算 score。 A PWM is the "shape of a lock cylinder" — some positions require a specific shape (G at position 2), others tolerate variants (position 6 accepts A or G). The TF is the "key" — only roughly matching keys fit. A motif scanner (e.g., FIMO) tries every DNA window as a "key" and scores the fit.

Motif Enrichment vs Footprinting

🔤 Motif Enrichment
  • 問題：peaks 有沒有某 TF 的 motif？
  • Question: Do peaks carry a TF's motif?
  • 答案：富集 yes/no（統計檢定）
  • Answer: enriched yes/no (stat test)
  • 方法：超幾何 / Fisher / binomial
  • Method: hypergeometric / Fisher / binomial
  • 工具：HOMER、MEME-ChIP、AME
  • Tools: HOMER, MEME-ChIP, AME
  • 含義：「可能結合」
  • Meaning: "potential binding"
VS
👣 Footprinting
  • 問題：motif 上是否有 TF 蹲坐？
  • Question: Is a TF actually sitting on the motif?
  • 答案：dip score / TF 佔比
  • Answer: dip score / TF occupancy
  • 方法：cut-site profile + HMM 或 score
  • Method: cut profile + HMM or score
  • 工具：HINT-ATAC、TOBIAS、Wellington
  • Tools: HINT-ATAC, TOBIAS, Wellington
  • 含義：「實際結合」
  • Meaning: "actual binding"
比喻： Motif enrichment 像「停車場有沒有畫車位」——只證明「可以停車」。Footprint 像「車位上有沒有車」——證明「真的停了」。第一層 cheap (序列掃描)，第二層 expensive (要原始 cut 訊號)。 Motif enrichment is "does the parking lot have lines?" — proves cars could park. Footprinting is "is a car actually there?" — proves cars did. First is cheap (sequence scan); second is expensive (raw cut signal).

# 3. 結果 (knownResults.html / homerResults.html)

  > annotated.tsv
# MEME-ChIP — 一站式 (de novo + known + footprint preview)
# 取 peaks ±100 bp 的 fasta

# 子工具： MEME (de novo), STREME, FIMO (scan), CentriMo (中心化分析)

library(BSgenome.Hsapiens.UCSC.hg38)

# 1. peak × cell counts (SummarizedExperiment)

counts <- addGCBias(counts, genome = BSgenome.Hsapiens.UCSC.hg38)

# 2. 取 JASPAR motifs + 掃 peaks

# 3. compute deviations (per-cell per-motif z-score)

# 4. variability ranking

head(vd[order(-vd$variability), ])

# 5. 視覺化 (TF activity on UMAP)

# 1. ATACorrect — 修正 Tn5 sequence bias
TOBIAS ATACorrect \
  --bam shifted.sorted.bam \
  --genome hg38.fa \
  --peaks union_peaks.bed \
  --blacklist hg38_blacklist.bed \
  --outdir tobias_out/sample1

# 2. ScoreBigwig — 每個 bp 算 footprint score
TOBIAS ScoreBigwig \
  --signal tobias_out/sample1_corrected.bw \
  --regions union_peaks.bed \
  --output tobias_out/sample1_footprint.bw

# 3. BINDetect — 跨條件比較 TF 結合差異
TOBIAS BINDetect \
  --motifs JASPAR2022_human.jaspar \
  --signals condA_footprint.bw condB_footprint.bw \
  --genome hg38.fa --peaks union_peaks.bed \
  --outdir bindetect_out/

# 結果 (bindetect_results.txt)：每個 motif 列 differential score、p-value
# HINT-ATAC (RGT 套件) — HMM-based footprint calling
# 1. 估計 atac bias model

  --output-location=hint_out/ \

  sample1_peaks.narrowPeak shifted.sorted.bam

# 輸出: sample1_footprints.bed (HMM 認為高信賴的 footprint 區段)

# 2. motif matching on footprints

  --organism hg38 --output-location matched/

# 3. differential footprint between conditions
rgt-hint differential --organism hg38 \

真實案例

真實案例：T 細胞耗竭

🛡️ 癌症免疫治療的染色質根源

Pauken KE, Sammons MA, Odorizzi PM et al. Science 2016, 354:1160–5; 與 Sen DR et al. 2016 同期。

背景：癌症 PD-1 阻斷療法對部分病人有效但對許多人失敗，原因在 T 細胞「耗竭」(exhaustion)。耗竭 T 細胞在表觀層級已被「鎖死」——即使 PD-1 阻斷，也無法完全恢復。

實驗：在 LCMV 慢性感染小鼠模型中，分離 effector T 細胞 (Teff) vs 耗竭 T 細胞 (Tex)，做 ATAC-seq + RNA-seq。

分析流程：

  1. MACS2 call peaks → union peak set
  2. DiffBind 找 DAR：Tex 特異開放區 ~6,000 個
  3. HOMER findMotifsGenome.pl 跑 motif enrichment
  4. TOBIAS BINDetect 比較 Teff vs Tex footprints

關鍵發現：

  • Tex 特異 enhancer 富集 NFAT、TOX、NR4A1/2/3 motifs。
  • Footprint 分析證實 TOX 確實在這些區域結合（不只是 motif 存在）。
  • 後續實驗證實 TOX 是耗竭 master regulator——剔除 TOX 可逆轉耗竭。
  • 解釋為什麼某些病人 PD-1 阻斷失敗：染色質已被 TOX 重塑、即使 PD-1 信號阻斷亦無法回頭。

影響：把 TOX 推上抗癌免疫治療「next-generation target」的舞台，啟發數個臨床試驗。展示了「ATAC + motif + footprint」做 mechanistic discovery 的範本。

Background: PD-1 blockade works in some patients but fails in many — T-cell "exhaustion" locks the epigenome, so even PD-1 release doesn't fully restore function.

Experiment: Chronic LCMV mouse model; sort effector T cells (Teff) vs exhausted T cells (Tex); ATAC-seq + RNA-seq.

Pipeline:

  1. MACS2 call peaks → union peak set
  2. DiffBind for DARs: ~6,000 Tex-specific open regions
  3. HOMER findMotifsGenome.pl motif enrichment
  4. TOBIAS BINDetect for Teff vs Tex footprints

Key findings:

  • Tex-specific enhancers enriched for NFAT, TOX, NR4A1/2/3 motifs.
  • Footprints confirmed TOX actually binds these regions (not just motif presence).
  • Follow-up experiments: TOX is the exhaustion master regulator; TOX KO reverses exhaustion.
  • Explains why some patients fail PD-1: chromatin already rewired by TOX, irreversible by PD-1 alone.

Impact: Put TOX on the map as a "next-generation immunotherapy target"; spawned several clinical trials. The template for "ATAC + motif + footprint" mechanistic discovery.

陷阱

5 個常見錯誤

用全基因組當 background：HOMER 預設 background 是 GC-matched 隨機區域。若你的 peaks 整體 GC 偏高，未做 match 會造成 GC-rich motif (如 SP1) 假富集。HOMER 預設一般可用，但客製分析建議用「另一組非 DAR peaks」當 background。Using whole genome as background: HOMER's default uses GC-matched random regions. If your peaks are high-GC and you don't match, GC-rich motifs (e.g., SP1) get spuriously enriched. HOMER's default is usually fine, but customise with "non-DAR peaks" for sensitive analyses.
沒做 Tn5 sequence bias 校正就 footprint：Tn5 對特定 9-mer 有 ±1.5× 偏好。未校正會把 bias 誤判為 footprint。TOBIAS ATACorrect、HINT-ATAC bias 校正是必做。Footprinting without Tn5 sequence-bias correction: Tn5 has ±1.5× preference for specific 9-mers. Without correction, the bias masquerades as footprint. TOBIAS ATACorrect / HINT-ATAC bias correction is mandatory.
把 motif enrichment 當作 TF binding 證據：motif 富集只說「peak 序列裡有這個模式」；並不代表此 TF 此時此地真有結合。要確認結合，需 footprint 或 ChIP-seq 驗證。Treating motif enrichment as binding evidence: enrichment only says "the pattern is there"; it does not prove a TF is bound in this context. Confirm with footprinting or ChIP-seq.
motif family 重疊未拆解：bZIP 家族（FOS、JUN、ATF）motif 幾乎相同。HOMER 結果第一名是「AP-1 family」，你不能直接說「是 FOS」——需要結合 expression 或 ChIP 驗證。Ignoring motif-family redundancy: bZIP family (FOS, JUN, ATF) share near-identical motifs. HOMER's top hit may be "AP-1 family" — you cannot conclude "FOS specifically" without expression or ChIP cross-validation.
footprint dip 在短 motif 上很小：motif 越短（如 6 bp），TF 蛋白 footprint 也越窄；dip 訊號弱、容易誤判。對於 < 8 bp motif，footprint 解讀要保守。Tiny footprints on short motifs: shorter motifs (e.g., 6 bp) → narrower TF footprint → weak dip, easy to misjudge. Be cautious for motifs < 8 bp.
多層次解釋

footprint dip 的物理意義

TF 蹲在 motif 上，Tn5 切不下去，所以該位置 read 變少——這就是 dip。

A TF squats on the motif and blocks Tn5; reads drop there — that's the dip.

考慮一個 motif 中心被 TF 蛋白佔據 ~10 bp 範圍。即使 motif 兩翼仍開放（Tn5 可切），中心 ~10 bp 被擋住。Cut-site profile 上看到：兩翼 cut density 高（flanks），中心一個 4-15 bp 寬的「下凹」(footprint dip)。Dip 深度 ∝ TF 在群體中的 binding 佔比 + binding 持續時間。

A TF occupies ~10 bp at the motif centre. Flanks remain open (Tn5 cuts there), but the central ~10 bp is occluded. The cut-site profile shows high flanking density plus a 4–15 bp central "dip" (footprint). Dip depth ∝ TF occupancy × residence time across the population.

數學上：cut density f(x) ≈ uniform_open_baseline − occupancy(x) · TF_block(x)，其中 TF_block(x) 是 TF 接觸地圖的卷積。HINT-ATAC 用 HMM 估計三種狀態的後驗（NFR baseline、footprint、boundary）；TOBIAS 用 BINDetect score = log2(open_neighbour / footprint_centre) 比較條件。Tn5 sequence bias 是干擾項：若 motif 含 Tn5 偏好序列，bias 會讓 dip 看起來更深（誤判）或更淺（漏判）。所以 ATACorrect 對特定 9-mer 的 Tn5 偏好做 k-mer 校正必不可少。值得注意：高度動態 TF（如 bZIP，residence time ~秒）的 footprint 比 stable TF (如 CTCF，residence ~分) 弱很多——這也是為什麼 CTCF 在 ATAC 上 footprint 最容易看到。

Mathematically: cut density f(x) ≈ baseline − occupancy(x) · TF_block(x), where TF_block(x) is the contact-map convolution. HINT-ATAC uses an HMM with three states (NFR baseline, footprint, boundary). TOBIAS BINDetect score = log2(open_flanks / footprint_centre), comparable across conditions. Tn5 sequence bias confounds: if the motif contains a Tn5-preferred 9-mer, the dip can appear inflated (false positive) or attenuated (false negative). ATACorrect's k-mer bias correction is therefore essential. Note: dynamic TFs (bZIP, residence ~seconds) produce far weaker footprints than stable TFs (CTCF, residence ~minutes) — which is why CTCF footprints are easiest to see in ATAC.

Papers often run both as sensitivity tests — overlap is most trustworthy.
數值意義上是 p 接近 0，但 HOMER 不做嚴格 FDR。當 peak 數很大（> 10,000），微小的 effect size 也會給超低 p。看 fold enrichment 與 % targets 才是真檢驗。Numerically yes (p ≈ 0), but HOMER's FDR is lenient. With many peaks (> 10,000), even tiny effects give huge -log(p). The real check is fold enrichment + % targets.
可：用 CIS-BP 跨物種推論（基於 TF protein domain），或自己 de novo（MEME-ChIP STREME）。JASPAR 含植物、線蟲、果蠅等較多動物 motif，但小動物較少。Yes: CIS-BP for cross-species inference (TF protein domain-based), or de novo (MEME-ChIP STREME). JASPAR covers plants, worm, fly, but rare for small animals.
經驗法則：> 500 peaks 跑 known enrichment；> 2,000 跑 de novo (MEME-ChIP)。少於 500 統計力會不足。Rule of thumb: > 500 peaks for known enrichment; > 2,000 for de novo (MEME-ChIP). Below 500 → low statistical power.
每位置 IC 都低（字母矮）= motif 不夠 informative，可能因 (a) binding sites 太少；(b) TF 本身 binding 弱；(c) motif 太短。Low IC at every position (short letters) = motif uninformative; likely (a) too few binding sites, (b) weak TF, or (c) motif too short.
原因：(a) TF 在群體中佔比低；(b) TF residence time 短；(c) Tn5 bias 未校正；(d) motif 短。解：增 depth、做 ATACorrect、focus 在 CTCF/CEBP 等 strong-footprint TF。Reasons: (a) low occupancy in the population, (b) short TF residence, (c) uncorrected Tn5 bias, (d) short motif. Fixes: deeper sequencing, ATACorrect, focus on strong-footprint TFs (CTCF, CEBP).
