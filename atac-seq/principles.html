為何重要

從生化機制理解 ATAC，才能正確解讀數據

許多人把 ATAC-seq 當「黑盒子」——丟 fastq 進 nf-core，拿 peaks 出來看。但當 QC 異常時，唯一能診斷的方式就是回到生化原理：為什麼 chrM 高？為什麼片段太短？為什麼 NFR 峰消失？這些都源於 Tn5 與染色質的交互作用。

這一章從分子層次建立你對 ATAC 的物理直覺：Tn5 二聚體如何「跳」進開放 DNA、9-bp 切位為何需要 +4/-5 校正、為什麼片段呈現 NFR + 核小體 ladder、以及 2013 原始版到 2017 omni-ATAC 的關鍵改良。

Many treat ATAC-seq as a black box — fastq in, peaks out via nf-core. But when QC fails, only the underlying biochemistry diagnoses the problem: why is chrM high? why are fragments too short? why did the NFR peak vanish? Each is a downstream consequence of Tn5–chromatin physics.

This chapter builds your molecular intuition: how the Tn5 dimer "jumps" into open DNA, why the 9-bp cut needs +4/-5 correction, why fragments form an NFR + nucleosomal ladder, and what changed between the 2013 original and 2017 omni-ATAC.

生化原理

Tn5 轉位酶機制

Tn5最初來自 E. coli 細菌轉位元 IS50，1990 年代由 Reznikoff 與 Goryshin 工程化為「hyperactive」變體，效率提升 1000 倍。 是來自細菌的轉位酶。工程化版本（含突變 E54K、M56A、L372P）對 DNA 有極高親和力，且攜帶 Illumina 接頭預載序列。它以二聚體形式工作：兩個 Tn5 monomer 各抓一條 DNA 鏈，同時完成：

  1. 切割：在兩條 DNA 上錯位切出 9 bp 「staggered cut」（forward 切位比 reverse 早 9 bp）。
  2. 嵌入接頭：把預載的 Read1 + Read2 adapter 兩端嵌入切口——這個動作叫 tagmentationtag + fragmentation。一步完成片段化+接頭嵌入，跳過傳統 ChIP-seq 的 sonication + ligation。（tag + fragmentation）。

關鍵點：Tn5 對 沒被核小體蓋住 的 DNA 親和力高出 10–100 倍。這是 ATAC 訊號的物理來源——不是 Tn5「主動避開」核小體，而是核小體在物理上擋住了它。

Tn5Originally from E. coli IS50 transposon; engineered to "hyperactive" by Reznikoff & Goryshin in the 1990s — efficiency boost ~1000×. is a bacterial transposase. The engineered version (mutations E54K, M56A, L372P) has high DNA affinity and carries preloaded Illumina adapters. It works as a dimer: two Tn5 monomers grab the two strands and simultaneously:

  1. Cut: staggered 9-bp cut on the two strands (forward cut 9 bp upstream of reverse cut).
  2. Insert adapters: preloaded Read1 + Read2 adapters insert into the gap — tagmentationtag + fragmentation — fragmentation and adapter ligation in one enzymatic step, skipping sonication + ligation used in classical ChIP-seq. (tag + fragmentation).

Key: Tn5 affinity is 10–100× higher for DNA not occluded by nucleosomes. This is the physical origin of ATAC signal — Tn5 doesn't "avoid" nucleosomes actively; nucleosomes physically block it.

比喻： 想像 Tn5 是一對拿著膠帶的「雙人舞者」——他們手牽手跳上 DNA 雙螺旋，同時切下兩條鏈、把膠帶（adapter）貼上去。只有 DNA 雙螺旋「空著」（沒被核小體裹住）他們才跳得進去。 Picture Tn5 as a "tandem dancer pair" carrying tape — they jump onto the DNA helix, cut both strands together, and stick the tape (adapter) on. They can only land where the helix is "empty" (not wrapped around a histone).

9-bp offset → +4 / -5 shift
常被誤解：+4/-5 shift 不是要修正 PCR bias 或定序誤差，而是修正 Tn5 兩條切位之間的 9-bp 物理偏移。沒做 shift，footprinting 的 bp 級解析度就會錯位。對 peak calling 影響小（區域層級），但對 motif footprint 影響大。 Common misconception: +4/-5 shift is not a PCR-bias or sequencing-error correction — it corrects the 9-bp physical offset between the two Tn5 cut sites. Without it, footprinting loses bp-level resolution. Peak calling is barely affected (region-level), but motif footprints are.
片段大小

片段大小分佈：鋸齒山脈

ATAC paired-end reads 還原出每個 fragment 的長度後，畫成直方圖，會出現經典的「四疊紀」結構：

  • <100 bp：NFRNucleosome-Free Region — Tn5 切在兩個相鄰核小體之間或進入 NDR 內。 片段——兩個 Tn5 落點都在同一個 NFR 內。最豐富。
  • ~180 bp：mono-nucleosome 片段——兩個 Tn5 落點各在一個核小體兩側（包住整個 ~147 bp 核小體 + linker）。
  • ~360 bp：di-nucleosome 片段——夾住兩個核小體。
  • ~540 bp / ~720 bp：tri-、tetra-nucleosome（罕見）。

10.5-bp ripple：放大來看，<200 bp 區段會出現 ~10.5 bp 週期的小波動——這是 DNA 雙螺旋每圈 10.5 bp 的痕跡（DNA 接觸核小體表面的旋轉相位）。

After reconstructing fragment lengths from paired-end reads, the histogram shows a classic 4-tier structure:

  • <100 bp: NFRNucleosome-Free Region — both Tn5 sites land in a single nucleosome-depleted region. fragments — both Tn5 cuts within one NFR. Most abundant.
  • ~180 bp: mono-nucleosomal — Tn5 cuts on either side of one nucleosome (~147 bp + linker).
  • ~360 bp: di-nucleosomal — spans two nucleosomes.
  • ~540 bp / ~720 bp: tri-, tetra-nucleosomal (rare).

10.5-bp ripple: zoom into <200 bp and a ~10.5-bp periodicity appears — the helical pitch of DNA wrapping the nucleosome surface.

比喻： 想像染色質是一串「珠子（核小體）+ 線（linker DNA）」。Tn5 只能切「線」，不能切「珠子」。隨機落兩刀：兩刀都落在同一段線 → 短片段 (NFR)；分別落在一個珠子的兩側 → ~180 bp；兩個珠子兩側 → ~360 bp。直方圖的階梯，就是這串珠子的指紋。 Chromatin is "beads (nucleosomes) on a string (linker DNA)." Tn5 can only cut string, not beads. Random pairs of cuts: both on same string → short (NFR); flanking one bead → ~180 bp; flanking two beads → ~360 bp. The histogram ladder is the fingerprint of that necklace.

必做後處理

ATAC 比對後的 5 個必做步驟

+4 / -5 shift

forward read 起點 +4，reverse read 起點 -5。alignmentSieve --ATACshift（deepTools）或 samtools view + awk 自己刻。對 peak calling 影響小，對 footprint 影響大。

Forward read start +4, reverse start -5. Use alignmentSieve --ATACshift (deepTools). Negligible for peaks, critical for footprints.

移除 chrM

粒線體 DNA 完全開放、無核小體 → Tn5 大量切 → 經典 30–80% reads 浪費在 chrM 上。samtools view ... | grep -v chrM 或 omni-ATAC 在實驗端用 DNase 預處理。

Mitochondrial DNA is fully open, nucleosome-free → Tn5 cuts everywhere → 30–80% of reads classically wasted on chrM. Filter: samtools view | grep -v chrM, or omni-ATAC pretreats with DNase.

移除黑名單

ENCODE blacklist 含已知 mapping-artifact 區（repeats、衛星 DNA、低映射性）。bedtools intersect -v -a bam -b hg38.blacklist.bed。

ENCODE blacklist marks mapping-artifact regions (repeats, satellites, low-mappability). bedtools intersect -v -a bam -b hg38.blacklist.bed.

去 PCR 重複

picard MarkDuplicates 或 samtools markdup，再用 -F 1024 過濾。記錄 PBC1（Library complexity）作為 QC。

picard MarkDuplicates or samtools markdup, then filter with -F 1024. Track PBC1 (library complexity) as QC.

品質過濾

samtools view -q 30 -f 2 -F 1804：保留 MAPQ≥30、properly paired、移除 unmapped/secondary/dup。

samtools view -q 30 -f 2 -F 1804: keep MAPQ≥30, properly paired; remove unmapped/secondary/dup.

真實案例

真實案例：Buenrostro 2013

🧪 ATAC-seq 誕生的那一篇

Buenrostro JD, Giresi PG, Zaba LC, Chang HY, Greenleaf WJ. Nat Methods 2013, 10:1213–8. SRA: SRR891268. ENCODE: ENCSR000EMT

背景：2013 之前，研究染色質開放性需要 ~10⁶ 細胞做 DNase-seq 或 FAIRE-seq，且實驗複雜。Stanford 的 Howard Chang & Will Greenleaf 實驗室問：能否用單一酵素一步搞定？

實驗：50,000 顆 GM12878（B 細胞淋巴瘤系），加入工程化 Tn5、tagmentation 30 分鐘、PCR 11 cycles，總共 ~半天。

關鍵發現：

  • 50k 顆細胞的 ATAC-seq 結果與 ~10⁷ 細胞的 DNase-seq 峰位重合度 > 0.9。
  • 第一次清楚看到 NFR + 180 bp + 360 bp 核小體 ladder。
  • 透過 motif 富集，重現 GM12878 知名 TF（PU.1、IRF、CTCF）。
  • 同篇示範首個「footprint 分析」：CTCF motif 周圍清楚看到 cut-site 凹陷。

影響：論文發表後 4 年內被引用 >2000 次，把染色質景觀帶入「少細胞、快實驗」時代。為 scATAC、空間 ATAC、TCGA 410 樣本研究等鋪路。

Background: Before 2013, studying chromatin accessibility required ~10⁶ cells with DNase-seq or FAIRE-seq, with complex protocols. Stanford's Chang & Greenleaf labs asked: can a single enzyme do it in one step?

Experiment: 50,000 GM12878 cells (B-cell lymphoma line), engineered Tn5, 30-min tagmentation, 11-cycle PCR — about half a day total.

Key findings:

  • 50k-cell ATAC peaks overlap > 0.9 with 10⁷-cell DNase-seq peaks.
  • First clear visualisation of the NFR + 180 bp + 360 bp nucleosomal ladder.
  • Motif enrichment recovered classical GM12878 TFs (PU.1, IRF, CTCF).
  • Same paper demonstrated the first ATAC footprint: clear dip in cut signal around the CTCF motif.

Impact: Cited >2,000 times within 4 years; brought chromatin profiling into the "few-cells, fast-protocol" era. Paved the way for scATAC, spatial ATAC, and TCGA's 410-sample atlas.

陷阱

5 個最常犯的錯

沒做 +4/-5 shift 就跑 footprinting：footprint 的 dip 中心會偏移 4–5 bp，導致與 motif 不對齊。對 peak calling 影響不大，但 footprinting 必做。Skipping +4/-5 shift before footprinting: the footprint dip shifts by 4–5 bp and won't align to the motif. Mostly harmless for peak calling, but mandatory for footprinting.
chrM% 過高未排除就 normalize：若 60% reads 在 chrM，扣掉後 effective depth 大降。應 先 移除 chrM，再用剩下的 reads 計算 depth/normalize。Normalising without removing chrM first: if 60% of reads are chrM, effective depth plummets. Remove chrM first, then compute depth / normalize.
用 single-end 跑 ATAC：無法重建 fragment-size，無法做 NFR/nucleosomal 分離。永遠 paired-end。Running single-end ATAC: you cannot reconstruct fragment sizes — no NFR / nucleosomal separation. Always paired-end.
過度 PCR 循環：建議 ≤ 12 cycles（omni-ATAC 用 qPCR 監控前段，找 ⅓ 飽和點停手）。過頭會導致 PBC1 暴跌、library 多樣性差。Too many PCR cycles: stay ≤ 12 (omni-ATAC monitors with qPCR and stops at ⅓ saturation). Overshoot tanks PBC1 / library complexity.
冷凍細胞當新鮮細胞跑：冷凍融解會增加細胞破裂與 chrM 釋放；應用 omni-ATAC 並提高 DNase 預處理比例。Treating frozen cells like fresh: freeze-thaw raises cell rupture and chrM release. Use omni-ATAC with stronger DNase pretreatment.
程式碼

實作範例

# 1. Adapter trimming (Trim Galore or fastp)
fastp -i R1.fq.gz -I R2.fq.gz \
  -o R1.tr.fq.gz -O R2.tr.fq.gz \
  --detect_adapter_for_pe --thread 8

# 2. Alignment with Bowtie2 (ENCODE ATAC standard)
bowtie2 --very-sensitive -X 2000 --dovetail -p 12 \
  -x bowtie2_idx -1 R1.tr.fq.gz -2 R2.tr.fq.gz \
  | samtools sort -@ 8 -o raw.bam -

# 3. Mark + remove PCR duplicates
picard MarkDuplicates I=raw.bam O=dedup.bam M=dup.txt REMOVE_DUPLICATES=true

# 4. Filter: MAPQ ≥ 30, properly paired, no chrM, no unmapped/secondary
samtools view -h -b -q 30 -F 1804 -f 2 dedup.bam \
  | grep -v -P 'chrM|chrUn|random|chrEBV' > filt.bam
samtools index filt.bam

# 5. Remove ENCODE blacklist (hg38)
bedtools intersect -v -abam filt.bam \
  -b ENCFF356LFX_hg38_blacklist.bed > clean.bam
samtools index clean.bam
# 6. Tn5 +4/-5 shift (deepTools alignmentSieve)
alignmentSieve --bam clean.bam -o shifted.bam --ATACshift \
  --filterMetrics filt.log
samtools sort -@ 8 -o shifted.sorted.bam shifted.bam
samtools index shifted.sorted.bam

# 7. Fragment-size histogram (deepTools)
bamPEFragmentSize -b shifted.sorted.bam \
  --histogram fragSize.png --plotTitle "ATAC fragment-size" \
  --maxFragmentLength 1000

# 8. Coverage track (bigWig, CPM-normalized)
bamCoverage -b shifted.sorted.bam -o sample.bw \
  --binSize 10 --normalizeUsing CPM \
  --effectiveGenomeSize 2913022398 -p 8 --extendReads

多層次解釋

為什麼要 +4/-5 shift？三種深度

一句話：Tn5 切了 9 bp 錯位的雙股切口，所以 read 起點不是真正的「中心」——往內挪 4 或 5 bp 才對。

One sentence: Tn5 makes a 9-bp staggered cut, so the read start isn't the true "centre" — nudge inward by 4 or 5 bp to fix.

Tn5 二聚體在 DNA 上下兩條鏈分別切開——上鏈切位比下鏈切位前移 9 bp。Illumina 接頭嵌入後，定序得到的 forward read 起點 = 上鏈切位，reverse read 結束 = 下鏈切位。要讓兩條 read 都對齊到「真正的 Tn5 中心位置」（兩切位中點），forward +4 bp、reverse -5 bp（4+5=9）。對 peak calling 影響很小（區域層級），但對 motif 中心 footprint 解析至關重要。

The Tn5 dimer cuts the two strands at offset positions — top-strand cut is 9 bp upstream of bottom-strand cut. After adapter insertion, sequenced forward-read start = top-strand cut; reverse-read end = bottom-strand cut. To align both to the "true Tn5 centre" (midpoint), shift forward +4 and reverse -5 (4+5=9). Tiny effect on peak calling, huge effect on bp-level footprint resolution.

從生物物理：Tn5 的二聚體結構決定了兩個活性位點之間的距離 ≈ 9 bp，這在 Davies et al. (2000) 的 Tn5 晶體結構就已揭示。實務上 ENCODE 的標準是 forward +4、reverse -5（Adey et al. 2010 提出的修正）。也有人用 +5/-4 或對稱 +4.5（不切實際因 BAM 為整數座標）。理論上完美 shift 應依 Tn5 catalytic site offset 的 9 bp 來決定，但生物變異性下 +4/-5 是 community 共識。對於 TF footprint 而言，若不做 shift，cut-site profile 的最大值會錯置於 motif 中心 ±4 bp，導致 TOBIAS / HINT-ATAC 對 motif 對齊失敗——這是為什麼 footprinting 必做 shift 的最終原因。

From biophysics: the Tn5 dimer's two catalytic sites are ~9 bp apart, as resolved in Davies et al. (2000) Tn5 crystal structure. ENCODE standard: forward +4, reverse -5 (Adey et al. 2010). Some use +5/-4 or symmetric +4.5 (impractical with integer BAM coords). The 9-bp catalytic offset is the theoretical basis; +4/-5 is community consensus. Without shift, the cut-site density maxima sit ±4 bp off motif centre, disrupting TOBIAS / HINT-ATAC motif alignment — the final reason footprinting must shift.
