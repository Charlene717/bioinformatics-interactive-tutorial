為什麼重要

從 reads 推 variants 的本質

當你看到一個位點上有 10 條 reads，其中 4 條顯示 A、6 條顯示 G——這個樣本的真實 genotype 是什麼？是 ref/alt heterozygous？還是 ref/ref + 4 個 sequencing errors？還是 alt/alt + 4 個 errors？

這就是 variant calling 的核心——一個機率推理問題：給定觀察 (reads)，找最可能的 genotype。本章將揭開 HaplotypeCallerGATK 旗艦 germline caller；在 active region 內做 local de novo assembly，再用 PairHMM 算 read-haplotype likelihood。、Mutect2GATK somatic caller；建立 tumor-normal 比較 + Panel of Normals + germline AF prior。、DeepVariantGoogle 開發；把 read pileup 轉成圖像，用 CNN 分類 genotype。2018 PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge 冠軍。 三大主流方法的核心數學。

You see a site with 10 reads: 4 show A, 6 show G. What's the true genotype? ref/alt heterozygous? ref/ref + 4 sequencing errors? alt/alt + 4 errors?

This is the heart of variant calling — a probabilistic inference problem: given observations (reads), find the most likely genotype. This chapter dissects the core math behind HaplotypeCallerGATK's flagship germline caller; local de novo assembly within active regions, then PairHMM for read-haplotype likelihoods., Mutect2GATK somatic caller; builds tumor-normal contrast + Panel of Normals + germline AF prior., and DeepVariantBuilt by Google; turns read pileup into images and classifies genotypes with a CNN. Won the 2018 PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge..

核心概念

Genotype Likelihood 公式

對二倍體一個位點，可能的 genotype 為 G ∈ {AA, AC, AG, AT, CC, CG, ...}（共 10 種，含 ref/alt 4 鹼基組合）。Bayesian 推論：

For a diploid site, possible genotypes are G ∈ {AA, AC, AG, AT, CC, CG, ...} (10 combos including all base pairs). Bayesian inference:

P(G | reads) ∝ P(reads | G) · P(G)
P(reads | G) = ∏i P(readi | G)
P(readi | G=AB) = ½ · P(readi | A) + ½ · P(readi | B) Posterior ∝ Likelihood × Prior · het is mixture of two homozygous likelihoods

每個 read 的 P(read | base) 由 base quality 決定：P(observe X | true X) = 1 − 10−Q/10。Prior P(G) 從 dbSNP / Hardy-Weinberg 而來。

P(read | base) per-read comes from base quality: P(observe X | true X) = 1 − 10−Q/10. Prior P(G) is from dbSNP / Hardy-Weinberg.

把 caller 想像成醫師看 X 光：10 條 reads 是 10 張不同角度的 X 光片，每張清晰度 (BQ) 不同。醫師 (caller) 結合「片子的內容」+「這種病在族群的盛行率 (prior)」算出「最可能的診斷 (genotype)」。
A caller is like a doctor reading X-rays: 10 reads are 10 X-rays at different angles, each with different clarity (BQ). The doctor combines image content + disease prevalence in the population (prior) to derive the most likely diagnosis (genotype).
Germline vs Somatic

兩條路線的本質差異

🧬 Germline

  • VAF 分布：離散 0% / 50% / 100%（Hardy-Weinberg）
  • Prior：強 (dbSNP, Hardy-Weinberg)
  • 樣本設計：單樣本或 cohort joint genotyping
  • 失敗模式：het 被叫成 hom (depth 不均)；low-complexity indel 漏報
  • 主要工具：HaplotypeCaller、DeepVariant、Strelka2 (germline)
  • VAF distribution: discrete 0% / 50% / 100% (Hardy-Weinberg)
  • Prior: strong (dbSNP, HW)
  • Design: single or cohort joint genotyping
  • Failure modes: het called as hom (uneven depth); low-complexity indels missed
  • Tools: HaplotypeCaller, DeepVariant, Strelka2 (germline)
VS

🦠 Somatic

  • VAF 分布：連續 0–50%（subclonality），常 < 10%
  • Prior：弱（不能假設 Hardy-Weinberg）
  • 樣本設計：paired tumor-normal，或 tumor-only + PoN
  • 失敗模式：FFPE artifact (C→T)、orientation bias (G→T)、germline 混入
  • 主要工具：Mutect2、Strelka2 (somatic)、VarScan2、MuSE
  • VAF distribution: continuous 0–50% (subclonality), often < 10%
  • Prior: weak (no HW assumption)
  • Design: paired tumor-normal, or tumor-only + PoN
  • Failure modes: FFPE artifact (C→T), orientation bias (G→T), germline contamination
  • Tools: Mutect2, Strelka2 (somatic), VarScan2, MuSE

HaplotypeCaller 深入

HaplotypeCaller 四步驟

Active Region 偵測

掃描全基因組，找「有 mismatch / soft-clip / indel 訊號」的小視窗 (~300 bp)。其餘區域跳過，速度提升 10–20×。

Scan the genome for small windows (~300 bp) with mismatch / soft-clip / indel signals. Skip the rest — 10–20× speedup.

局部組裝

在 active region 內，用 reads 建 de Bruijn graph把 reads 切成 k-mers 後構成的圖；路徑表示可能的 haplotypes。GATK 預設 k = 10, 25。，列出所有候選 haplotypes。這一步讓 HaplotypeCaller 對 indel 與 cluster mutation 特別強。

Within each active region, build a de Bruijn graphA graph of k-mers from reads; paths represent candidate haplotypes. GATK uses k = 10, 25 by default. from reads and enumerate candidate haplotypes. This is why HaplotypeCaller excels at indels and clustered mutations.

PairHMM

PairHMMPair Hidden Markov Model；考慮 match/mismatch/insertion/deletion 三種狀態的 HMM，計算「read 來自此 haplotype 的機率」。是 GATK 最耗時的步驟，常用 SIMD 加速。 算 P(read | haplotype)，視 base quality 與 indel quality 而定。

PairHMMPair Hidden Markov Model — an HMM with match/mismatch/insertion/deletion states that computes P(read | haplotype). The most compute-heavy step in GATK; often SIMD-accelerated. computes P(read | haplotype), driven by base and indel qualities.

Genotype 機率推論

有了 read-haplotype likelihood，可推 P(reads | genotype = h1/h2) = ∏i ½ · [P(readi | h1) + P(readi | h2)]。最後比較所有 (h1, h2) 組合，輸出最高機率 genotype + Phred-scaled GQ。

With read-haplotype likelihoods, P(reads | genotype = h1/h2) = ∏i ½ · [P(readi | h1) + P(readi | h2)]. Compare all (h1, h2) pairs, output the max-probability genotype + Phred-scaled GQ.

GVCF 魔法

GVCF + Joint Genotyping

傳統做法：每樣本單獨 call VCF，最後 merge。問題：

  • 真陰位 vs 缺失：樣本 A 在 chr1:1000 是 ref/ref，VCF 不會記錄。樣本 B 在同位是 het。Merge 後 A 變成「缺失」，但其實 A 是有 reads 但呼叫 ref/ref。
  • 聯合 calibration 失效：無法做 cohort-wide VQSR、無法檢測 Mendelian violation。

Traditional approach: per-sample VCFs, merge at end. Problems:

  • True-negative vs missing: Sample A is ref/ref at chr1:1000 — not in VCF. Sample B is het. After merge, A becomes "missing" though it was actually covered and called ref/ref.
  • No joint calibration: cohort-wide VQSR and Mendelian-violation checks become impossible.

GVCF (Genomic VCF) = 每樣本記錄所有位置，包括 ref-only：

  • 變異位置：與普通 VCF 同
  • 非變異區：用 <NON_REF> 描述 ref block，內含「若有變異會多少 likelihood」資訊

下游 GenotypeGVCFs 合併所有 GVCF，聯合呼叫每個位點，把 cohort-level 訊息納入 prior。

GVCF (Genomic VCF) records every position per sample, including ref-only:

  • Variant positions: as in normal VCF
  • Non-variant: described as ref blocks via <NON_REF> with likelihoods conditioned on hypothetical alt alleles

Downstream GenotypeGVCFs merges all GVCFs and jointly calls each site, incorporating cohort-level signal into the prior.

GVCF 為了不爆容量，將連續 ref 位置「壓縮」為 ref blocks（同 GQ band 合併）。所以 GVCF 比 BAM 小一個量級，比普通 VCF 大 3–5×。

大族群 (>1000 樣本)：建議用 GenomicsDBImport 而非 CombineGVCFs，速度與記憶體都更友善。

cohort-wide 不可省：同一族群 50 個樣本一起 genotyping，比 50 個獨立 call 後合併，多偵測 ~3% 真陽性 (低 VAF / borderline het)。

GVCFs compress consecutive ref positions into ref blocks (sharing GQ bands) to avoid bloat. GVCFs are an order of magnitude smaller than BAM, but 3–5× larger than a typical VCF.

Large cohorts (>1000): use GenomicsDBImport instead of CombineGVCFs — far better memory and speed.

Cohort-wide is not optional: jointly calling 50 samples vs merging 50 independent calls detects ~3% more true positives (low VAF / borderline het).

  -ERC GVCF \
  --pair-hmm-implementation AVX_LOGLESS_CACHING \
  -O HG002.g.vcf.gz

DeepVariant 拆解

CNN 的勝出邏輯

DeepVariant 的核心發想：把每個候選位點的 read pileup 轉成 RGB 圖像，再用 Inception-v3 CNN 分類 (homozygous_ref / het / hom_alt)。

  • Channels：read base、base quality、mapping quality、strand、insertion/deletion 等
  • Pixels：每 read 一橫條，每 reference 位置一豎欄
  • 訓練：用 GIAB truth set 訓練；轉移學習適應不同平台

優勢：CNN 能自動學到傳統 HMM 寫不出來的微妙模式（如「strand bias + low BQ + 同 cycle 位置」三特徵組合的 artifact）。在 GIAB benchmark 上 SNV F1 ~99.95%。

DeepVariant's core idea: turn each candidate site's read pileup into an RGB image, then classify (hom_ref / het / hom_alt) with an Inception-v3 CNN.

  • Channels: read base, base quality, mapping quality, strand, insertion/deletion
  • Pixels: one row per read, one column per reference position
  • Training: on GIAB truth set; transfer-learnable across platforms

The CNN learns subtle joint patterns that HMMs cannot capture (e.g., "strand bias + low BQ + same-cycle artifact" three-way combo). On GIAB, SNV F1 ~99.95%.

DeepVariant 訓練模型：WGS、WES、PacBio HiFi、ONT、Hybrid 各有專屬模型。錯用 (例 WGS 模型跑 WES) 會 F1 掉 1–2%。
DeepVariant model checkpoints: WGS, WES, PacBio HiFi, ONT, Hybrid each have dedicated models. Using wrong (e.g., WGS model on WES) drops F1 by 1–2%.
Mutect2 + Somatic

Mutect2 paired tumor-normal

Mutect2 的核心：tumor 出現的變異 + normal 沒有 + 不在 PoN + AF prior 低 = 高機率 somatic。

四個核心元件：

  • PoNPanel of Normals — 用同 protocol 30+ 個 normal 樣本建的 VCF，標記「技術產生的」recurring artifacts。每個實驗室應建自己的 PoN。：標記 recurring technical artifacts
  • germline AF prior：用 gnomAD AF 當作「這位點是 germline」的 prior，避免把 het germline 誤判 somatic
  • contamination model：估計 tumor 中 normal 細胞（含 germline）的混入比例
  • orientation bias：FFPE 樣本 C→T、氧化造成 G→T，需 F1R2 / F2R1 read orientation 校正

Mutect2's core: present in tumor + absent in normal + not in PoN + low AF prior ≈ high somatic probability.

Four key components:

  • PoNPanel of Normals — built from 30+ normals run on the same protocol, marks recurring technical artifacts. Every lab should build its own PoN.: flags recurring technical artifacts
  • germline AF prior: uses gnomAD AF as "is this germline?" prior, preventing germline het being called somatic
  • contamination model: estimates fraction of normal cells (carrying germline) in tumor
  • orientation bias: FFPE C→T and oxidation G→T artifacts; corrected via F1R2 / F2R1 read orientation
# 1. 主呼叫（含 PoN + germline AF prior）
gatk Mutect2 \
  -R GRCh38.fa \
  -I tumor.bam -tumor patient01_T \
  -I normal.bam -normal patient01_N \
  --panel-of-normals pon.vcf.gz \
  --germline-resource af-only-gnomad.vcf.gz \
  -O somatic.vcf.gz \
  --f1r2-tar-gz f1r2.tar.gz

# 2. 校正方向偏差
gatk LearnReadOrientationModel \
  -I f1r2.tar.gz -O orient.tar.gz

# 3. 估計汙染
gatk GetPileupSummaries -I tumor.bam \
  -V af-only-gnomad.vcf.gz -L exons.bed -O tumor.pileups.table
gatk CalculateContamination \
  -I tumor.pileups.table -O contamination.table

# 4. 過濾
gatk FilterMutectCalls \
  -V somatic.vcf.gz -R GRCh38.fa \
  --contamination-table contamination.table \
  --ob-priors orient.tar.gz \
  -O somatic.filtered.vcf.gz
# 建 PoN 三步驟（用 30+ 個 normal）
# 1. 每 normal 跑 Mutect2 tumor-only 模式
for nm in normal1 normal2 ... normal30; do
  gatk Mutect2 -R GRCh38.fa -I $nm.bam -O $nm.pon.vcf.gz --max-mnp-distance 0
done

# 2. GenomicsDBImport 合併
gatk GenomicsDBImport -R GRCh38.fa --genomicsdb-workspace-path pon_db \
  -V normal1.pon.vcf.gz -V normal2.pon.vcf.gz ...

# 3. 建 PoN VCF（保留出現在 ≥ 2 個 normal 的變異）
gatk CreateSomaticPanelOfNormals -R GRCh38.fa \
  -V gendb://pon_db --min-sample-count 2 -O pon.vcf.gz
# Tumor-only：當沒有配對 normal 時（很有挑戰）
gatk Mutect2 \
  -R GRCh38.fa \
  -I tumor.bam \
  --panel-of-normals pon.vcf.gz \
  --germline-resource af-only-gnomad.vcf.gz \
  -O tumor_only.vcf.gz

# tumor-only 必加嚴格過濾：
gatk FilterMutectCalls -V tumor_only.vcf.gz \
  --max-events-in-region 2 \
  --min-allele-fraction 0.05 \
  -O tumor_only.filtered.vcf.gz

# 警告：tumor-only 大量 germline 殘留，需 gnomAD AF < 0.001 過濾

案例

TCGA · PrecisionFDA 案例

TCGA · 2006–2018 · 11,000 cases · 33 cancers

案例 1：TCGA 揭開癌症體細胞圖譜

TCGA (The Cancer Genome Atlas, NCI/NHGRI) 對 11,000 個腫瘤-normal 配對做 WES/WGS/RNA-seq，發現：

  • 不同癌症的 driver mutation 數量差距達 1000 倍（兒童白血病 ~5 個 vs 黑色素瘤 ~500 個）
  • 50% 癌症有 TP53 突變；BRAF p.V600E 在黑色素瘤 50% 出現 → vemurafenib 標靶藥物誕生
  • 同類組織不同病人有「molecular subtype」，如乳癌的 luminal A/B、HER2+、basal-like → 治療策略個別化

關鍵生物資訊：MC3 (Multi-Caller Consensus) 用 Mutect2 + VarScan + MuSE + ... 七個 caller 取交集 → 公開高信心 somatic call set。

TCGA (NCI/NHGRI) sequenced 11,000 tumor-normal pairs across 33 cancers (WES/WGS/RNA-seq), revealing:

  • Driver counts differ 1000× across cancers (pediatric AML ~5 vs melanoma ~500)
  • 50% of cancers carry TP53; BRAF p.V600E in 50% of melanomas → vemurafenib targeted therapy
  • Same-tissue tumors show "molecular subtypes" — luminal A/B, HER2+, basal-like in breast cancer — personalizing treatment

Bioinformatics highlight: MC3 (Multi-Caller Consensus) used Mutect2 + VarScan + MuSE + ... seven callers, intersection released as the high-confidence somatic set.

Google · 2017 · DeepVariant · PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge

案例 2：DeepVariant 的崛起

2017 年 PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge 的目標：用 GIAB 樣本評估 caller 準確度。Google 提交 DeepVariant，以「不寫一行 genomics 邏輯，純粹用 CNN 學」打敗所有手寫 caller，獲 SNV 最高 F1。

影響：(1) 開啟「ML-first」變異偵測時代；(2) 證明 genomics 可用 image-classification 範式；(3) 引出 DeepSomatic、Clair3 (ONT)、PEPPER 等系列工具；(4) Google 開源 DeepVariant 後，Illumina DRAGEN、Nvidia Parabricks 都集成。

反思：DeepVariant 不解釋為什麼某位點被判 het，給臨床審查帶來新的挑戰——「黑盒可信度」的爭論至今未息。

The 2017 PrecisionFDA Truth Challenge evaluated callers on GIAB samples. Google submitted DeepVariant: with no hand-coded genomics logic — pure CNN — it beat every hand-engineered caller and won the SNV F1.

Impact: (1) opened the "ML-first" variant calling era; (2) proved genomics works as image classification; (3) seeded DeepSomatic, Clair3 (ONT), PEPPER; (4) once open-sourced, was integrated into Illumina DRAGEN and Nvidia Parabricks.

Reflection: DeepVariant doesn't explain why a site is het — challenging for clinical review. The "black-box trustworthiness" debate continues.

陷阱與 tips

常見陷阱

跳過 GVCF 直接 -ERC NONE：單樣本快但喪失 cohort joint genotyping 的所有好處。50 樣本族群這樣做 → 漏 ~3% 真陽性。
Skipping GVCF with -ERC NONE: faster per-sample but loses all cohort joint-genotyping benefits. A 50-sample cohort misses ~3% true positives.
Mutect2 沒給 PoN：少了 technical artifact 過濾，FP 暴增 5–10×。即使建 PoN 用 5 個 normal 也比沒有好。
Mutect2 without PoN: losing technical artifact filtering means 5–10× more FPs. Even a 5-normal PoN beats none.
不分 WES / WGS 都用 -ERC GVCF 全基因組掃：WES 應加 -L exons.bed --interval-padding 100，否則 90% 時間花在 off-target。
WES without intervals: always add -L exons.bed --interval-padding 100 for WES; otherwise 90% compute is wasted off-target.
並行加速：用 --intervals 分 chr 並行跑（24 工作分到 24 cores），最後 MergeVcfs 合併。WGS 從 24 小時降到 1.5 小時。
Parallelize: split by chromosome with --intervals across 24 cores, then MergeVcfs. WGS drops from 24 h to ~1.5 h.

Cross-caller consensus: for critical regions (drivers, disease panels), intersect two independent callers (HaplotypeCaller + DeepVariant). FP < 0.1%.

GIAB benchmark：DeepVariant SNV F1 ≈ 99.95%，HaplotypeCaller ≈ 99.7%；indel F1 DeepVariant 99.7% vs HC 99.4%。但 DeepVariant 是 black box；臨床判讀仍偏好 HC（每個 INFO 欄位都可解釋）。實務上：研究用 DV，臨床用 HC 或兩者交集。
GIAB: DeepVariant SNV F1 ≈ 99.95%, HaplotypeCaller ≈ 99.7%; indel DV 99.7% vs HC 99.4%. But DV is a black box; clinical labs often prefer HC (every INFO field explainable). Practical: DV for research, HC or intersection for clinical.
cohort > 50 樣本：joint 比 single-sample merge 多偵測 2–5% TP（多為低 GQ het）。trio 用 PossibleDeNovo 標記 de novo SNV (germline mutation rate ~1.2 × 10⁻⁸ per bp per generation)。
Cohort > 50: joint detects 2–5% more TP than single-sample merge (mostly low-GQ hets). Trio joint allows PossibleDeNovo annotation (germline mutation rate ~1.2 × 10⁻⁸ per bp per generation).
tumor-only 模式 + 大 PoN (≥ 50 個 normal) + 嚴格 gnomAD AF < 0.001 過濾。準確度約損失 30% TP，VUS 比例 2–3 倍。臨床診斷不建議；研究 driver landscape 可接受。
Tumor-only + large PoN (≥ 50 normals) + strict gnomAD AF < 0.001 filter. ~30% TP loss, 2–3× more VUS. Not recommended clinically; acceptable for research driver landscapes.
如果 tumor 有 20% normal 細胞混入（cellularity 80%），真 somatic VAF 50% 的變異會被看到 ~40% — 仍可叫。但真正受影響的是 germline het (VAF 50%) 看起來像 somatic low VAF (~10%)。contamination model 估計這比例後校正 likelihood，避免 germline 被誤判 somatic。
If tumor has 20% normal-cell contamination (cellularity 80%), a true somatic VAF 50% looks like ~40% — still callable. The real danger: germline het (VAF 50%) can look like somatic low-VAF (~10%). The contamination model estimates the fraction and corrects likelihoods to avoid germline-as-somatic errors.
GVCF 把連續 ref-only 位置壓成 ref block（如 chr1:100-500），共享相同 GQ band（如 GQ ≥ 30）。讀 GVCF 看 END INFO 欄位知 block 範圍；<NON_REF> alt 表示「若該位有未觀察的變異，其 likelihood 是這樣」。
GVCFs compress consecutive ref-only positions into ref blocks (e.g., chr1:100-500) sharing a GQ band (e.g., GQ ≥ 30). The END INFO gives block range; <NON_REF> alt encodes "if a hypothetical variant existed here, its likelihood would be...".
FFPE 固定造成 cytosine deamination → C→T (G→A on opposite strand) artifact，常 VAF 5–15%、單 strand 出現 → 直接看像 low-VAF somatic。Mutect2 的 --f1r2-tar-gz + LearnReadOrientationModel 校正：「真 somatic」應該 F1R2 和 F2R1 兩方向都出現；artifact 只一邊。
FFPE fixation causes cytosine deamination → C→T (G→A on opposite strand) artifacts at VAF 5–15%, single-stranded — looks like low-VAF somatic. Mutect2's --f1r2-tar-gz + LearnReadOrientationModel fixes this: true somatic appears on both F1R2 and F2R1; artifacts only on one.
2024 Google 發布 DeepSomatic，paired tumor-normal CNN-based caller，benchmark 與 Mutect2 持平且 indel 略勝。但 PoN 概念被 model training 內化，使用者不需自建 PoN。
In 2024 Google released DeepSomatic, a paired tumor-normal CNN caller — benchmarks match Mutect2 with slightly better indels. PoN logic is internalized in the model; users don't build PoN.
