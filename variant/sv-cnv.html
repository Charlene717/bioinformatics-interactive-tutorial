SV / CNV 偵測

大片段變異——SNV/Indel 之外的另一個世界。臨床診斷率提升的關鍵。

Large-scale variants — another world beyond SNV/Indel. Key to improving clinical diagnostic yield.

🟢 Beginner🟡 Intermediate🔴 Advanced🔵 Clinical
開場故事

為什麼還要做 SV / CNV？

一個現實：罕見遺傳病的傳統 WGS + SNV/Indel calling 診斷率約 30-40%。這代表有 60% 病人即使做了全基因組定序，仍找不到致病變異。其中很大部分是 SV/CNV——例如：

  • BRCA1/BRCA2 large deletions：佔遺傳性乳癌約 10-15% 病例
  • 22q11.2 deletion syndrome：1/2000 新生兒、心臟畸形 + 顎裂 + 免疫缺陷
  • FMR1 CGG repeat expansion：脆性 X 症候群、智能障礙
  • SMN1 exon 7 deletion：脊髓性肌肉萎縮症 (SMA)

這些變異用 SNV caller 完全看不到。所以 SV/CNV 偵測不是「進階選項」，而是任何完整臨床基因組分析的標配。2024 年的趨勢：long-read 技術讓 SV 偵測敏感度從 short-read 的 50% 提升到 95%+。

A reality: traditional WGS + SNV/Indel calling diagnoses only 30–40% of rare-disease patients. That means 60% of patients, even after whole-genome sequencing, have no causative variant identified. Much of that is SV/CNV — for example:

  • BRCA1/BRCA2 large deletions: ~10–15% of hereditary breast cancer cases
  • 22q11.2 deletion syndrome: 1/2000 newborns; heart defects + cleft palate + immune deficiency
  • FMR1 CGG repeat expansion: Fragile X syndrome; intellectual disability
  • SMN1 exon 7 deletion: Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA)

These variants are invisible to SNV callers. So SV/CNV calling isn't "optional advanced" — it's standard in any complete clinical genomic analysis. 2024 trend: long-read sequencing boosts SV sensitivity from short-read's ~50% to 95%+.

類比：SNV 像「找文章中的錯字」、SV/CNV 像「找文章中整段被刪除或重複的段落」。前者很重要但只佔錯誤的一部分；後者一漏就嚴重曲解原意。
Analogy: SNVs are like finding typos; SV/CNVs are like finding deleted or duplicated paragraphs. Both matter, but missing a paragraph fundamentally distorts the meaning.
核心概念

一、SV vs CNV：定義與類型

結構變異 (SV)Structural Variant. > 50 bp 的大片段變化。涵蓋 DEL、DUP、INS、INV、TRA、BND。 是 > 50 bp 的大片段變化，包括 deletion (DEL)、duplication (DUP)、insertion (INS)、inversion (INV)、translocation (TRA)、breakend (BND)。CNVCopy Number Variant. SV 的子集，特指 DEL 與 DUP，數量變化以 kb 至 Mb 計。 是 SV 的子集，特指 DEL 與 DUP，數量變化以 kb 至 Mb 計。

Structural Variants (SVs)> 50 bp large variants: DEL, DUP, INS, INV, TRA, BND. are > 50 bp changes: deletion (DEL), duplication (DUP), insertion (INS), inversion (INV), translocation (TRA), breakend (BND). CNVsCopy Number Variants — subset of SVs covering DEL and DUP at kb–Mb scale. are a CNV subset of SVs covering DEL/DUP at kb–Mb scale.

偵測訊號

二、四種偵測訊號

1. Read depth (RD)

DUP 區域 reads 翻倍、DEL 區域 reads 減半——對 CNV 最直觀。工具：CNVkit、Control-FREEC、GATK gCNV。

DUP doubles depth, DEL halves it — most intuitive for CNV. Tools: CNVkit, Control-FREEC, GATK gCNV.

2. Read pair (RP)

paired-end 之間距離若超出預期或方向倒置，暗示 DEL / INV。

Abnormal paired-end distance/orientation suggests DEL / INV.

3. Split read (SR)

read 一半比對 chr1、一半比對 chr5 → translocation 或 INS 的標記。

Half a read maps to chr1, half to chr5 → translocation or insertion marker.

4. De novo assembly

無法比對的 reads 組裝後與基因組比對，找到 novel INS / INV。

Unmapped reads assembled and re-aligned — finds novel INS / INV.

案例研究

四、BRCA1 大片段缺失

Walsh et al. 2010 · PNAS · BROCA panel

📂 SNV-only 漏掉的 12%

真實資料 Walsh 等人對 360 個有家族病史的乳癌患者做基因檢測。傳統 Sanger + SNV-only 找到致病變異 26%。加入 BRCA1/BRCA2 大片段缺失（用 MLPA / 後來的 NGS-CNV）後，診斷率躍升至 38%。

關鍵發現：BRCA1 exon 13 duplication 與 exon 22 deletion 是建立者突變 (founder mutation)——在 Ashkenazi Jewish 與某些拉丁裔族群有 1-3% 攜帶率。沒做 CNV 分析的家族檢測，等於漏掉了這些高致病性變異。

啟示：2024 年 ACMG 建議：所有遺傳性癌症 panel 都應同時做 SNV + CNV 分析。最便宜的方式是用 short-read WGS + CNVkit；中高端用 long-read。

Real Data Walsh et al. tested 360 breast-cancer probands with family history. Sanger + SNV-only identified pathogenic variants in 26%. Adding BRCA1/BRCA2 large deletions (via MLPA, later NGS-CNV) raised yield to 38%.

Key finding: BRCA1 exon 13 duplication and exon 22 deletion are founder mutations — 1–3% carrier rate in Ashkenazi Jewish and some Latino populations. Family testing without CNV analysis misses these highly pathogenic variants.

Takeaway: 2024 ACMG: all hereditary cancer panels should include SNV + CNV analysis. Cheap option: short-read WGS + CNVkit; high-end: long-read.

五、實作範例

configManta.py \
  --bam analysis_ready.bam \
  --referenceFasta GRCh38.fa \
  --runDir manta_run/
manta_run/runWorkflow.py -j 8
# 輸出在 manta_run/results/variants/diploidSV.vcf.gz
# 1. 計算 read counts
gatk CollectReadCounts \
  -I sample.bam -L exons.preprocessed.interval_list \
  --interval-merging-rule OVERLAPPING_ONLY \
  -O sample.counts.hdf5
# 2. 群體式 CNV calling
gatk GermlineCNVCaller \
  --input sample1.counts.hdf5 ... \
  --contig-ploidy-calls ploidy_calls/ \
  --output cnv_out/ --run-mode COHORT
cnvkit.py batch \
  tumor.bam --normal normal.bam \
  --targets exons.bed --annotate refFlat.txt \
  --fasta GRCh38.fa \
  --output-reference my_reference.cnn \
  --output-dir cnvkit_out/
cnvkit.py scatter -s sample.cns sample.cnr -o sample.scatter.png
# Long-read SV calling — PacBio HiFi 或 ONT
sniffles --input long_read.bam \
  --vcf output.vcf.gz \
  --tandem-repeats human_GRCh38_no_alt_analysis_set.trf.bed \
  --threads 8

FAQ

七、常見問答

若你的臨床診斷率仍低 (< 40%) 且懷疑 SV 比例高（如智能障礙、自閉症等神經發育疾病），long-read 顯著提升敏感度。成本：PacBio HiFi 30× 約 $800、Nanopore 約 $500（2024 價）。

If your clinical yield is < 40% and you suspect high SV burden (e.g., intellectual disability, autism spectrum), long-read significantly boosts sensitivity. Cost: PacBio HiFi 30× ~$800; Nanopore ~$500 (2024).

建議分開：CNV 用 read depth + segmentation，重點在 dose 變化；SV 用 RP/SR/assembly，重點在 breakpoint。臨床報告應分項列出。但在內部分析中，可用 SURVIVOR 等工具合併以避免重複報告同一變異。

Recommend separate: CNV via depth + segmentation (focus on dosage); SV via RP/SR/assembly (focus on breakpoints). Clinical reports should list separately. For internal analysis, SURVIVOR can merge to avoid duplicate reporting.
