TRACK 4 · VARIANT CALLING

變異偵測 (WGS / WES)

從 FASTQ 到可信變異——GATK Best Practice 帶你走完每個步驟，並理解每個決策背後的統計、生物與臨床意涵。

From FASTQ to trusted variants — walk through GATK Best Practice while understanding the statistics, biology, and clinical reasoning behind every decision.

🟢 Beginner 🟡 Intermediate 🔴 Advanced 🔵 Clinical
為什麼重要

從一個鹼基，到一條人命

一個 SNVsingle-nucleotide variant；單核苷酸變異，最常見的變異形式，全人類基因組約有 4–5 百萬個。就足以決定一個 4 歲孩子是健康成長還是飽受 cystic fibrosis 折磨；一個 BRCA1 移碼突變就能改變一名 30 歲女性是否要做預防性乳房切除術；一個 BRAF p.V600E 體細胞突變就能決定黑色素瘤病人能否獲得 vemurafenib 的續命機會。

變異偵測 (Variant Calling) 是整個臨床基因組學、藥物基因組學、群體遺傳學、癌症精準醫療的共同入口。做錯了，下游的所有結論都是空談；做對了，一份 VCF 檔可以延長壽命、避免錯誤手術、解開家族 30 年的謎團。

本軌的目標是幫助你：(1) 看懂每個 GATK Best Practice 步驟為什麼存在；(2) 理解 germline vs somatic 流程的統計差異；(3) 培養出 看一份 VCF 就能挑出毛病 的眼力；(4) 學會臨床等級的 ACMG 變異分類思維。

One SNVsingle-nucleotide variant — the most common form of variation, about 4–5 million per human genome. can decide whether a 4-year-old grows up healthy or suffers cystic fibrosis. One BRCA1 frameshift can decide whether a 30-year-old woman has prophylactic mastectomy. One somatic BRAF p.V600E can decide whether a melanoma patient gets life-extending vemurafenib.

Variant calling is the shared entry point for clinical genomics, pharmacogenomics, population genetics, and precision oncology. Do it wrong and every downstream conclusion is hollow; do it right and a single VCF can extend lives, avert wrong surgeries, and solve 30-year family mysteries.

This track aims to help you: (1) understand why every GATK Best Practice step exists; (2) grasp the statistical differences between germline and somatic pipelines; (3) develop the eye to spot problems just by reading a VCF; (4) reach clinical-level ACMG variant-classification thinking.

類比

把流程想像成...

偵探重建犯罪現場：每條 read 是一個目擊者，有些目擊者近視 (low BQ)，有些位置不對 (misaligned)，有些重複作證 (PCR duplicates)。一個好的 variant caller 像優秀法醫：聽完所有目擊者後，用機率學重建犯罪 (variant)，並對不可靠的目擊者打折扣 (down-weight)。
A detective reconstructing a crime scene: each read is a witness — some near-sighted (low BQ), some misaligned, some repeating the same story (PCR duplicates). A good variant caller is a skilled forensic scientist: probabilistically reconstructs the event (variant) after hearing all witnesses, discounting the unreliable ones.
BQSR 像校正一把雜訊很大的尺：儀器標的「10 cm」其實在不同溫度、不同位置量出來的真實長度有偏差。BQSR 用「已知量好的物體 (dbSNP)」反推每種情境的偏差，再把尺重新刻度。
BQSR is like calibrating a noisy ruler: the instrument's "10 cm" mark actually measures slightly different real lengths at different temperatures and positions. BQSR uses objects of known length (dbSNP) to back-infer the bias in each context, then re-engraves the ruler.

兩條路線

Germline vs Somatic 主軸對比

🧬 Germline

  • 來源：遺傳自父母
  • VAF：離散 0% / 50% / 100%
  • 樣本設計：單樣本或 trio / cohort
  • 代表工具：HaplotypeCaller、DeepVariant
  • 下游：聯合 genotyping → VQSR
  • 臨床用途：遺傳病診斷、ACMG 分類
  • Origin: inherited from parents
  • VAF: discrete 0% / 50% / 100%
  • Design: single, trio, or cohort
  • Tools: HaplotypeCaller, DeepVariant
  • Downstream: joint genotyping → VQSR
  • Clinical use: Mendelian dx, ACMG
VS

🦠 Somatic

  • 來源：後天突變 (癌症)
  • VAF：連續 0–50%，常 < 10%
  • 樣本設計：paired tumor-normal 或 tumor-only + PoN
  • 代表工具：Mutect2、Strelka2、VarScan2
  • 下游：FilterMutectCalls、driver mut 分析
  • 臨床用途：標靶治療配對、MRD 監測
  • Origin: acquired (cancer)
  • VAF: continuous 0–50%, often < 10%
  • Design: paired tumor-normal or tumor-only + PoN
  • Tools: Mutect2, Strelka2, VarScan2
  • Downstream: FilterMutectCalls, driver analysis
  • Clinical use: targeted therapy match, MRD
範例資料

教學與基準公開資料集

📦 Genome in a Bottle truth set

NIST 標準樣本 HG001–HG007；HG002 (Ashkenazi Jewish son) 是 benchmark 業界標配，附 high-confidence VCF + BED。所有 caller 的 precision / recall 都會跟 GIAB 比。

NIST reference samples HG001–HG007; HG002 (Ashkenazi Jewish son) is the industry benchmark with high-confidence VCF + BED. Every caller compares precision/recall against GIAB.

🌍 1000 Genomes

2504 個個體、26 個族群的 WGS；first proof that 92% of variants in any individual are common (AF > 5%)。

2,504 individuals, 26 populations of WGS — first to show 92% of variants in any individual are common (AF > 5%).

📊 gnomAD v4 730k exomes

730k+ exome + 76k+ genome 的群體頻率資料庫，註釋必備。分 popmax (任一族群最大頻率)、nfe (Non-Finnish European)、afr、eas 等子集。

730k+ exomes + 76k+ genomes for allele frequency — essential for annotation. Subdivided into popmax, nfe (Non-Finnish European), afr, eas, and more.

🦠 TCGA / ICGC

11k+ tumor-normal 配對，33 種癌症。所有公開癌症 driver 與 mutational signature 的源頭。

11k+ tumor-normal pairs across 33 cancers. The source of all public cancer drivers and mutational signatures.

🏥 ClinVar

NIH 維護的臨床顯著變異庫；每個變異有 P/LP/VUS/LB/B 五級分類 + review status (0–4 stars)。臨床判讀必查。

NIH-maintained clinical variant database; each variant gets a P/LP/VUS/LB/B class + review status (0–4 stars). Mandatory for clinical interpretation.

💊 COSMIC + Hotspots

癌症體細胞突變目錄；COSMIC ID、CGC、cancer hotspots 標記重要驅動基因突變。

Catalog of somatic mutations; COSMIC ID, CGC, and cancer hotspots flag key driver mutations.

思考

開放性討論問題

Q1. 為什麼一個健康人「相對於參考序列」會有 4 百萬個變異？這是否意味著參考基因組「不正常」？

Q2. 同一個檢體用兩個 caller (HaplotypeCaller vs DeepVariant) 跑，得到的 VCF 不完全一樣——你信哪一個？「真實」(ground truth) 該如何定義？

Q3. 在臨床基因檢測，VUS (Variant of Uncertain Significance) 約佔 30%。對病人與家屬，「不確定」造成的心理負擔可能比「確定致病」更大——基因組學該如何處理？

Q4. 罕見族群 (如台灣原住民) 在 gnomAD 中代表性不足，會造成「在白人不罕見的變異被誤判為原住民的致病變異」。我們該如何補救？

Q1. Why does a healthy person have 4 million variants "vs the reference"? Does this mean the reference genome is abnormal?

Q2. Running the same sample through two callers (HaplotypeCaller vs DeepVariant) gives slightly different VCFs — which do you trust? How do we define ground truth?

Q3. In clinical testing, VUS (Variants of Uncertain Significance) make up ~30%. The psychological burden of uncertainty is often worse than confirmed pathogenicity — how should genomics handle this?

Q4. Underrepresented populations (e.g., Taiwanese indigenous groups) in gnomAD can cause variants common in those populations to be misclassified as pathogenic — what is the remedy?
